Is McCreamy Still In Misfits Podcast?

Posted by Billy Koelling on Saturday, August 17, 2024

Fans have assumed that McCreamy, a New Zealand-based gaming YouTuber, is no longer part of the Misfits podcast. Misfit is a group of six gaming creators based in Melbourne.

Misfits currently consist of six different gaming YouTubers and they are from America, Australia, and New Zealand.

The group has three channels: Misfits, Misfits Podcast, and Misfits Podcast Clips. The group even purchased a mansion in Melbourne.

Irrespective of posting content to their channel, the group members use the official Misfits channel to post the occasional vlogs. On the other hand, Misfits Podcast is a weekly podcast where the group has a conversation on different topics. 

The group has six members Fitz, iNotorious, McCreamy, SwaggerSouls, Toby on the Tele, and Zuckles. 

Is McCreamy Still In Misfits?

Misfits used to be around six different members but now there are only five members and people haven't seen McCreamy there. 

McCreamy is still active on his channel but seems to have disappeared from the group podcast. 

He has been a part of a podcast for a long time. Last time, the group jokingly made a video with a title on why McCreamy might go to prison. But the title was just clickbait, and many people fell for it. 

He has even jokingly been canceled at the group podcast.

McCreamy has not been a part of a podcast after COVID-19, as most sources have claimed. People think McCreamy is avoiding group contact because he has asthma and is trying to be cautious about his health. 

Another rumor was that McCreamy might have won a lottery and won 10 million dollars, which might have been why he left the podcast. 

Where Is McCreamy Now?

McCreamy is a famous YouTube comedian and a professional gamer from New Zealand.

McCreamny is quite famous for making a clan channel named xd. 

He joined YouTube back in 2016, and he is known for posting Overwatch comedic gameplay. He is mostly known for playing Fortnite and has even collaborated with YouTubers. 

Other than Fortnite, he also used to play Payer Unknown Battlegrounds (PUBG) and make a comedic video out of it before he permanently moved to Fortnite in 2017. 

He started xd Clan. It is a clan channel that allows anyone to join the game but gives more priority to the good players.

His YouTube channel has 8.9 million subscribers and views 1.9 billion. Annually, he earns $974,500 out of YouTube.
