Who is Jim Acosta? 

A Journalist is a prominent character for news networks as well as a face of television. They work effortlessly to represent and cover news from all over the world. Jim Acosta is an American journalist. He is also known as the former Chief White House Correspondent for CNN, where Jeff Zeleny also served as a senior White House Correspondent for CNN.

Jim Acosta was born as Abilio James Acosta on 17th April 1971 in Washington D.C., the United States of America. Born to his American parents, he is of White ethnicity and holds American nationality. Furthermore, there is no information about his family background. 

Talking about his education, Acosta joined Annandale High School on 1989. Later, he received his graduation in Mass Communication with a minor Political science via James Madison University in 1993. 

Acosta began his career as a radio personality. He served for WMAL based in Washington D.C. In 1994, he began working with WTTG-TV as a desk assistant. The following year, Acosta worked at WBIR-TV as a reporter, where is worked for three years and left in 1998.

Furthermore, Jim has also worked as a reporter for KTVT-TV. Besides, Jim has also worked as a correspondent for CBS news from 2003 to 2007. 

In late 2007, Acosta joined CNN and began covering presidential campaigns of Democratic candidates like Hillary Clinton and Barack Obama. He also appeared as a host on the show Ballot Bowl. In 2012, Jim was promoted and became National Political Correspondent for CNN. 

Additionally, Acosta is popular for covering ex-president Barack Obama's historic trip to the island in Cuba. Since 9th January 2018, Jim is serving as a Chief White House Correspondent for CNN.
