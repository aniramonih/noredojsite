Ray Mears Finance Overview

Career Journey and Milestones

He grew up in the North Downs of Southern England. His early life was marked by a keen interest in nature and survival skills.

Despite his ambition to join the Royal Marines, he couldn’t meet the eyesight requirements. This led him to a different path, where he founded Woodlore in 1983. Woodlore offers bushcraft-related courses and paraphernalia, which have been highly successful.

His television career began in 1994 with the BBC series Tracks. This was followed by Ray Mears’ World of Survival in 1997.

Over the years, Mears has presented numerous series, each showcasing his deep knowledge of survival techniques and bushcraft.

Sources of Income

Ray Mears’ income is derived from several sources:

  • Television and Media: His TV shows, including Ray Mears’ Bushcraft and Wild Britain with Ray Mears, have been significant contributors to his income. These shows not only entertain but also educate viewers on survival skills.
  • Books and Publications: Mears has authored several books, such as My Outdoor Life and Bushcraft Survival. These publications have added to his wealth through sales and royalties.
  • Business Ventures: Woodlore, his bushcraft company, plays a crucial role in his financial stability. Offering courses and selling survival gear, Woodlore has established a strong reputation in the market.
  • Public Speaking and Workshops: Mears also earns from public appearances and conducting survival workshops. These events attract a wide audience, eager to learn from his expertise.

His personal life has seen its share of ups and downs. He met his first wife, Rachel, in 1992, and they married in 2005.

FAQs About Ray Mears

Who is Ray Mears?

Ray Mears is a British woodsman, author, TV presenter, and instructor known for his expertise in bushcraft and survival skills.

He founded the Woodlore School of Wilderness Bushcraft in 1983, which has since become a leading institution for teaching these skills.

Ray’s numerous television series, including Tracks, World of Survival, and Ray Mears’ Bushcraft, have made him a household name globally.

What is Bushcraft according to Ray?

Bushcraft, as taught by Ray Mears, involves learning and mastering skills that allow individuals to live in and interact with the wilderness. It’s about practical knowledge, such as fire making, shelter building, and understanding the natural world.
