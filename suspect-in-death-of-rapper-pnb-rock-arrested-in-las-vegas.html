Suspect in death of rapper PnB Rock arrested in Las Vegas

Posted by Billy Koelling on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

Arrest Made in Las Vegas in Connection to Death of Rapper PnB Rock

The Los Angeles Police Department has made an arrest in relation to the death of rapper PnB Rock. Suspect Freddie Lee Trone, who was charged in the shooting incident alongside his 17-year-old son on Sept. 13, was apprehended on Thursday, September 29, at approximately 1 p.m.

The tragic incident took place at Roscoe’s House of Chicken ‘N Waffles in south Los Angeles while the rapper, whose legal name is Rakim Allen, was dining with his girlfriend. According to reports, Trone’s son was also taken into custody on Tuesday, September 27, in Lawndale, California.

Both Trone and his son are facing charges of murder, second-degree robbery, and conspiracy to commit robbery. Additionally, another individual, 32-year-old Shauntel Trone, was arrested in Gardena, California, and charged with being an accessory after the fact to the killing. The nature of her connection to the other defendants has not been clarified by LAPD.
