Understanding Prodigy’s Early Life and Career

Prodigy’s journey into the world of hip-hop began at a young age. Born in Hempstead, Long Island, he was introduced to the music industry through his family. His grandfather was a saxophonist, and his mother was a member of the girl group The Crystals. Prodigy’s early exposure to music played a significant role in his career choice.

Formation of Mobb Deep

Prodigy’s career took a significant turn when he met Havoc while attending the High School of Art and Design in New York City. Together, they formed Mobb Deep, which would become one of the most respected duos in hip-hop history. Their raw and unfiltered lyrics, combined with gritty beats, captured the essence of street life, earning them a dedicated fan base.

Breakthrough and Success

Mobb Deep’s breakthrough came with their second album, “The Infamous,” which included hits like “Shook Ones Pt. II” and “Survival of the Fittest.” This album cemented their place in hip-hop and contributed significantly to Prodigy’s net worth.

Prodigy’s Solo Career and Other Ventures
