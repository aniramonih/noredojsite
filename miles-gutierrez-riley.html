The Wilds Season 2 Actor Miles Gutierrez Riley Wiki | Bio

23-years-old Miles Gutierrez Riley (born on September 17, 1998) is a Mexican-African actor and playwriter from Bellflower, California. He has had a great career in the theatre industry, but in the TV industry, he debuted through an Amazon serial named “The Wilds Season 2,” where his role was of a 19 years old school student named Ivan Taylor.

His character Ivan Taylor is a dramatist and activist with exquisite dress sense and razor-sharp humor. However, his remarks may be piercing, sometimes too deeply, which has lost him friends along the road.

The young guy is working on another project named “On The Come Up.” It is a Sanaa Lathan-directed Paramount movie, the story of which revolves around a 16 years old girl named Briwho wants to become a rapper.

Childhood, Parents, and Education

On September 17, 1998, Miles Gutierrez Riley (age 23 years) came to this when he took birth at the house of his father and mother in Bellflower, California. His mom Sonia Gutierrez is a CSU graduate who currently lives and works in Los Angeles, California. Unfortunately, we have not been able to find anything regarding Miles except the fact that he is an African-American.

Riley spent most of his childhood in California, and he was a teenager when he joined Downey Theater. Here he got introduced to the art of acting, and his time at Downey helped him learn the basics of acting.

During the final year of school, he was clear about what he wanted to do for the rest of his life and to become a pro at acting; he joined Fordham University. At FU, he studied for BA in Theatre performance and graduated in 2020.

Career

While studying at Fordham University, Riley learned a lot about theatre art and scriptwriting. He actively participated in various plays like “Twelfth Night,” “Satellites,” “Antigonick,” “Cock,” “Ibsanity, or Aurora Fra Bergen,” “Action,” “Magnolia,” “Little Nemo in Slumberland,” “Desaparecido,” and “Fade to White.” Besides acting, he also writes poems and mixes American mythology with modern-day humor.

Miles also won a few awards for his excellent performances. For 2019-2020 he won Denzel Washington Scholarship from Fordham University, and in 2020, during Strawberry One-Act Festival, he got awarded Best Actor.

He is full of talent, and it did not take much time for the casting directors of “The Wilds” to recognize that he has the potential to become a great artist one day. Undoubtedly, Gutierrez performed exceptionally well in the Amazon series, and it is also true that he has a very bright future in the Hollywood industry.
