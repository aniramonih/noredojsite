Jayni Chase (Chevy Chase Wife) Age and Bio

Posted by Tandra Barner on Thursday, August 8, 2024

Notable actor and comedian, Chevy Chase is praised for his exemplary marital life with Jayni Chase. The duo has been together for 39 years and still going strong, unlike many other Hollywood couples. Hence, in this article, we write about Jayni, her age, husband, and biography. Please be with us till the end.

Jayni chase Age and Bio

Jayni Chase is widely known as actor Chevy Chase’s wife, but she can also be best described as an environmental activist. In fact, she is a multi-talented woman who is also a writer and conservation educator. As for her age, she was born on August 24, 1957, and is currently 64 years old. Being born in North Hollywood, Los Angeles, she is an American by nationality.

There is no detail regarding who she was born to, but we are sure her childhood was pretty good. She graduated from North Hollywood High School and later enrolled in the University of California Santa Barbara, where she pursued her communications degree. Apart from her educational background, there are minimal facts available about her on the internet. We don’t even know whether she has any siblings or not.

Jayni Chase is the Wife of Chevy Chase

Jayni is in a long-lasting marital relationship with actor Chevy Chase. But their love story might amaze you. They both found each other when they were not actually looking for a relationship to be in. In fact, they first met each other in one of Chevy’s film shootings, where Jayni was serving as a production assistant.

It is unknown from which year they started dating each other, but the duo took their love to the next level and tied their knots on June 19, 1982. Their family and friends witnessed their wedding ceremony in Pacific Palisades in Los Angeles, California. They have already spent almost four decades of marital life and are still going strong. Furthermore, their relationship is regarded as one of the long-lasting in the Hollywood Industry.

Jayni Chase has 3 daughters with Chevy Chase

With a four-decade-long marital relationship, Jayni shares three children with her husband, Chevy. After they tied their wedding knots in 1982, Jayni got pregnant in the same year and gave birth to their first child Cydney Cathalene Chase. As she was born on January 4, 1983, she is now 38 years of age. She is a professional singer and a songwriter who showcases her musical skills on social media. In addition, she is married to Ryan Bartell.

Furthermore, on January 19, 1985, the couple welcomed their daughter Caley Leigh Chase, currently 36. She followed her dad’s footsteps and has appeared in numerous films and TV shows since 2005. She’s also the Artistic Associate at the Huntington Theatre Company. Moreover, the loving couple still wanted to expand their family and welcomed their youngest child Emily Evelyn Chase on September 29, 1988. She is now a 33-year-old Instagram Influencer who is often vocal about criminal justice reform and many other burning topics.

Furthermore, Jayni is also a stepmother to a son that her husband conceived with his first wife. His name is Bryan, and he is currently 42. He was born on October 24, 1979; however, he is out of the limelight.

Net Worth and Career

Jayni has several accolades to her name. She is an actress, writer, and most importantly, an environmental activist. It has been two decades that she has been working in environmental community action and environmental education. She is well-known for her work in ‘National Lampoon’s Vacation,’ ‘The Annual Tel Aviv Gala Presents a Salute to Goldie Hawn,’ and ‘Biography.’ Furthermore, she is a contributor to Huffington Post and a founder of the Center of Environmental Education. She states that the 13-year-old Jayni was inspired to be an environmentalist when the U.S., for the first time, celebrated Earth Day in 1970.

Moreover, Jayni, along with her husband, founded Project Eco-School, designed to encourage environmental education. Likewise, she has also been awarded a respected Huge C. Lane Jr. Award of Excellence in Environmental Education. Furthermore, she might have earned a fortune from her career as an environmentalist and educator, but there are no details about it.

Husband Chevy Chase

Chevy Chase, 77, is a prominent American actor and a comedian. Besides, he is well-known for his Weekly Update section of Saturday Night Live. He first started his career in 1967 by co-founding comedy channel one. He then entirely focused on comedy as his full-time career by 1973. According to his IMDb, he has 100 credits as an actor. His most famous works include Foul Play, Hot Tub Machine, Orange County, CollegeHumor Originals, Community, and many more. One of his movies, The Ogilvy Fortune, is in the pre-production phase. He has been active from 1968 till the present.

Moreover, the actor also has 14 credits as a writer. He has further started his talk show in 1993 named “The Chevy Chase Show.” In addition, he has accumulated a net worth of around $60 million from his career as an actor, comedian, and writer.

Jayni Chase is diagnosed with Rheumatoid Arthritis

Unlike many others who fear the surgery, she was very excited about it. She also tried many remedies like Ayurveda herbs, exercises, and many more to get rid of her condition.
