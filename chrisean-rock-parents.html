Chrisean Rock Parents: Eugene Malone and Charla Malone

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

Chrisean Rock comes from a large family with parents Eugene Malone and Charla Malone, along with eleven siblings—four brothers and seven sisters.

The talented American singer and rap artist, Chrisean, is currently expecting her first child with her long-term partner, Blueface.

Earlier this year, Blueface faced criticism when he requested a paternity test to confirm his biological connection to the child Chrisean is carrying.

In December 2022, Chrisean revealed a shocking detail about her past, mentioning that she had previously undergone three abortions.

Despite her challenging experiences, Chrisean is thrilled about her current pregnancy and remains steadfast in her determination to embrace motherhood, regardless of Blueface’s support.

Starting her journey as an Instagram star, Chrisean Rock gained fame as an American musician and model, captivating over 3 million followers on the social media platform.

In her pursuit of a career in rap, she adopted the moniker “Chrisean Rock,” discarding her surname, “Malone,” for a more captivating persona.

Hailing from Baltimore, Chrisean has released several notable songs, including “Vibes,” “Lonely,” “Rainy Days,” “Lit,” “Keep Swimming,” and many more.

Chrisean Rock parents

Chrisean Rock, a reality TV star, was born on March 14, 2000, in Baltimore, Maryland.

Growing up, she spent most of her childhood in Baltimore, surrounded by her large and close-knit family.

Chrisean’s parents, Eugene and Charla Malone, have 11 other children, creating a bustling household for the 22-year-old.

According to Gossip Next Door, Chrisean’s parents reportedly went through a separation in 2022, which added to the dynamics within the family.

Viewers of the show “Blueface and Chrisean: Crazy In Love” witnessed the tensions between Blueface and Chrisean’s family, particularly her father.

The show became famous for the altercation between Chrisean’s father and Blueface.

Friction also arose between Blueface and his own family, as well as among other family members from both sides.

During an interview with B Simone in 2023, Chrisean mentioned that she maintains a close relationship with her parents.

However, she described herself as playing the role of both mother and father in their dynamic.

Chrisean expressed the need to accept the situation for what it is.

Chrisean further explained that she doesn’t feel her parents are heavily involved in her life at the moment.

Instead, she is focused on working hard and pursuing her ambitions, prioritizing her grind over familial dynamics.

Eugene Arthur Malone

Eugene Arthur Malone, the father of Chrisean Rock, was born in Maryland in 1968 and is presently 56 years of age.

He attended Forest Park High School in Baltimore and later pursued culinary arts to pursue a career as a chef.

Eugene currently works at Rip’s Country Inn in Bowie, Maryland.

Eugene was incarcerated during Rock’s early childhood and did not meet his daughter until she turned seven.

The specific details of his offense remain undisclosed.

Despite his previous involvement with the law, Eugene is determined to make amends for the lost time and actively engages in his children’s lives.

His social media biography indicates that he has renounced his past and transformed into a caring family man.

Charla Malone

Chrisean Rock Malone’s mother, Charla Malone, is currently 60 years old, having been born in 1964.

At the age of 32, Charla was arrested for theft charges, along with Eugene and a teenager.

While she hasn’t faced subsequent arrests, Charla has struggled with substance abuse issues.

Due to her drug addiction, Charla was absent during the early years of her daughter’s life.

When Chrisean brought her to Los Angeles, fans noticed certain concerning behaviors from Charla and questioned whether she was still engaged in drug use.

Malone is the mother of eleven kids.

Three of them were born before she met Eugene, but eight of her children are with him.

Chrisean Rock has a total of twelve siblings, including Terence Dorsey, Unique Dorsey, Tessa Malone, and eight others.

Among her siblings, Chrisean holds the position of the eleventh child in the family.

In terms of gender distribution, Chrisean has four brothers and seven sisters.
