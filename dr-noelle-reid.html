Dr. Noelle Reid Career

Prior to medical school served as a health administrative intern at the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services – MidWest division. She worked as a patient advocate at Evanston Northwestern Hospital through the Institute for Diversity and after graduating, Cum Laude, from Spelman College, joined AmeriCorps/”I Have A Dream” Foundation®. There she worked in Los Angeles coordinating after-school programs that aided in tutoring and instructing elementary students in reading, math, science, and dance.

Dr. Noelle owns Trinity Health and Wellness Center in Beverly Glen and firmly believes in an East meets West approach to medicine. Her duties as a family doctor encompass everything from giving pap smears and prostate exams to physicals and treating STD’s.In addition to her traditional medical practice, she also has a studio where she teaches yoga and meditation multiple times a week.
