You must have seen this vibrant and beautiful face in any of the Mafikizolo songs. The gorgeous queen with a magical voice is called Nhlanhla Nciza. Besides music, the latter has a life that most people do not know of. How about taking a trip down her biography.

Nhlanhla Nciza is not only a successful musician but also a fashion icon. Her fashion sense is interestingly unique and brings out the beauty of African culture. The latter has topped the headlines that do not relate to her music or fashion career. She has made headlines for separating with her husband of more than fifteen years. By now, you should be asking yourself why Nhlanhla Nciza and TK Nciza called it quits.

Nhlanhla Nciza biography

Nhlanhla Nciza is the female figure that most people look up to. She is famous for being a member of South Africa's award-winning music group; Mafikizolo that popular after releasing a single; 'Love potion,'. that earned more than two million views on YouTube. The queen is an image to reckon, especially since she has an exemplary fashion sense. You might be interested in knowing more details about her.

How old is Nhlanhla Nciza?

Nhlanhla Nciza was born on 15th March 1978. As of date, Nhlanhla Nciza age is forty-one years old. She, however, does not look anywhere close to it, especially for her statement fashion sense.

Nhlanhla Nciza husband

The multi-talented artist was married to TK Nciza. The full name of Nhlanhla Nciza husband is; Thembinkosi Nciza. Thembinkosi is a businessman and producer at TS Records executive. The two had been married for more than fifteen years before they split.

Nhlanhla Nciza daughter's death

Before the split, the marriage had yielded four children; three sons and their only daughter. The two lost their daughter Zinathi Nciza in through a tragic road accident that almost claimed Thembinkosi's mother.

Zinathi Nciza succumbed to the accident that occurred on 7th December 2009. Her death was a big blow to her family, especially her mother. Her bold smile was replaced with sadness. She even lost a reasonable amount of weight and sadness was the only language that she knew. In an interview that the latter did a year after the tragic death of her baby, the singer expressed her loss in her heart wrenching message saying; “We have made our peace with losing our little princess. We will always miss her but we are moving on.”

Even in her death, Nhlanhla speaks so fondly of her daughter. It goes without question that she loved her more than anyone could imagine. On her daughter's fifteenth birthday, Nciza penned down a touching message about how much she treasured her daughter and precious she was to her.

The touching message read, "She would be 15 today, our beautiful princess Zinathi. There so much I wanna say but the words fail me. Can't believe it's been 9 years. My heart is filled with so much pain as if it were yesterday when you left us."

"My heart is filled with so much pain as if it were yesterday when you left us. But in the midst of it all I, thank God for giving me the best 6 years of my life. I love you now and forever more my angel. Happy 15th birthday my Zinathi iNtsikelelo Nciza. MaRhadebe omhle, Ndlebentle zombini. Rest in Eternal Peace,"

Nhlanhla Nciza's wedding anniversary

Despite the painful loss of their daughter, Nhlanhla Nciza and her family have been tightly knitted. They have not shown any signs of marital issues. In fact, about two years ago, Nhlanhla Nciza and her husband hosted a party at their home, to celebrate their thirteenth marriage anniversary. On her special occasion, Nhlanhla Nciza donned a beautiful white dress and looked all glammed up. Her bold smile was proof of how happy and excited she was.

To crown her big day, Nhlanhla Nciza expressed the emotion on her Instagram page. In a post, she stated, “Today my heart is filled with so much joy, happiness and gratitude. Today my beloved and I are celebrating our anniversary. It could only be through God’s grace and the love we have one another that we have reached this milestone. I pray that our father continues to bless and protect our union. HAPPY 13 WEDDING ANNIVERSARY MY LOVE (sic).”

The momentous occasion was attended by some of their close friends, in their new house. Among those that were in attendance were; Thembi, Seete and Nthato Mashishi.

Nhlanhla Nciza's marriage split

On 20th June 2019, Nhlanhla Nciza Instagram was the centre of attraction for most people, especially after the artist took to her Instagram page to affirm that there was trouble in paradise and that she and her husband had separated. To set the record straight, she posted a picture of herself and her husband. The message in the caption said it all. It read; “After almost 15 years of our strong bond of marriage, my husband TK Nciza and I have come to a tough but amicable decision to bring an end to our union,”

She went further to state that she was grateful for the support that TK had given her and for the time they were married and for helping her raise their children.

To affirm that there was no bad blood between them, she stated; "We shall remain on good terms and continue our endeavor to be great and exemplary parents to our children,"

To wrap it up, Nhlahla thanked the public for their support. She also pleaded with the public to grant them privacy, especially for her kids.

The two, have, however, not gone the extra mile to explain further the reasons for their 'tough but amicable decision'.

The split comes a few days after Nhlanhla peened down a touching father's day message to TK.
