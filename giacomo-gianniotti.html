Giacomo Gianniotti - Bio, Age, Height, Net Worth, Facts, Nationality

Posted by Tandra Barner on Monday, August 19, 2024

Giacomo Gianniotti is a talented Italian-Canadian film and television actor. Giacomo Gianniotti came to fame for his role as Dr. Andrew DeLuca on the popular medical drama television series “Grey’s Anatomy.”

Early Life

Giacomo Gianniotti was born in Rome, Italy, on 19 June 1989. His full name is Giacomo Keaton Gianniotti and he holds Italian nationality. According to the astrological chart, he has a birth sign of Gemini and is currently 34 years old. He belongs to the Italian-Candian ethnicity while his religious beliefs are unknown.

Giacomo Gianniotti – Education

Moving on to his academic background, he attended high school at Cardinal Carter Academy for the Arts in Toronto. Later, he completed his graduation from Humber College’s Theatre Program. Similarly, he has completed an actor’s residency at Norman Jewison’s Canadian Film Centre in Toronto.

Professional Career

Giacomo Gianniotti made his acting debut, guest starred in an episode of the Italian television series “Medicina Generale” in 2010. In the year 2013, he continued appearing in several television shows such as “Beauty & the Beast” and “Copper” in which he starred in three episodes.

Similarly, he appeared in a recurring role as Lord Julien in the first season of “Reign.” From 2013-2014, he played Leslie Garland for several episodes in the Canadian TV series “Murdoch Mysteries.”

He played Freddy in the now-canceled television series “Selfie” (2014) and later appeared in the 2015 made-for-TV drama film “The Secret Life of Marilyn Monroe.” Between 2013 to 2014, he appeared in two Short films named ‘Done’ and ‘Still.’  He appeared in the 2015 television program “Backpackers” as Andrew.

In 2015, he played surgical intern Dr. Andrew DeLuca in the final two episodes of “Grey’s Anatomy’s” eleventh season. On January 8, 2016, he got promoted to series regular from seasons 12-17 and appeared in a total of 110 episodes. During her tenure in the series, he was also able to get nominated for a couple of awards. As a result, his acting career was boosted.

In 2016, he made his big screen debut, playing the role of Sam Stoller in the biographical sports film “Race.” He has made his recurring role in TV series “Murdoch Mysteries” (2013-2023), and “Station 19” (2018-2021). His other movie credits are “The Cuban” (2019), “Diabolik: Ginko Attacks!” (2022), and “Diabolik: Who Are You?” He also has lent his voice in video games such “Marvel’s Avengers” (2020) and “Horizon: Forbidden West” (2022).

Awards

Gianniotti’s performance on “Grey’s Anatomy” earned him the Tell-Tale TV Award for Favorite Actor in a Network Drama Series in 2021. In 2019, Gianniotti received one of the Top 25 Canadian Immigrant Awards presented by Canadian Immigrant Magazine. He has also been nominated for two Golden Maple Awards for his works in “Grey’s Anatomy.”
