Early Life

Future was born as Nayvadius DeMun Wilburn in 1983 on 20th November in Atlanta, Georgia. He was brought up in Atlanta, in a musically oriented family. Future completed his schooling from Columbia High School.

Personal life

As far as Future’s personal life is concerned, the rapper is always surrounded by ladies. He has four children, all with different mothers, which include Brittni Mealy, India J, Jessica Smith, and popular singer Ciara. Ciara and Future even engaged in 2013, but they separate their paths in 2014 because of Future’s infidelity.

In 2016, he was sued by two of his former partners Jessica Smith and Ciara. Jessica sued him for not paying child support and neglecting the child as a father, while Ciara sued him for libel, slander, and defamation.

Age, Height, and Weight

Being born on 20 November 1983, Future is 40 years old as of today’s date 21st July 2024. His height is 1.88 m tall, and his weight is 70 kg.

Career

Future started off his career with a musical group called ‘The Dungeon Family,’ which also inspired his stage name. He was motivated by his Rico Wade, his cousin and fellow group member, who inspired him to sharpen his musical skills and pursue a career in rapping.

Future officially started his career in 2009. He has worked with record labels like Freebandz, Epic, and A1. He also writes and produces some of his own songs.

In 2014-15 he released a trilogy of mixed tapes which included ‘Monster,’ ‘Beast Mode,’ and ’56 Nights’. This year he released two works ‘Future’ and ‘Hndrxx,’ both of which debuted at number one position on 200 US Billboards charts. Some of his other popular music albums also include ‘Evol,’ ‘DS2’, and ‘What a Time to be Alive.’

Future has worked with artists like Gucci Mane, The Weeknd, Young Thug, Rocko, Mike Will Made-It, Jeezy, Drake, and Zaytoven, etc. In 2016, Future was seen on with Drake ‘Summer Sixteen Tour’. Recently he also appeared in a single from Taylor Swift’s album ‘Reputation.’

Awards & Achievements

Future is a Grammy-nominated singer who has been nominated for several other awards like ‘American Music Award,’ ‘BET Award,’ ‘BET Hip Hop Awards,’ ‘Billboard Music Awards,’ ‘iHeartRadio Music Award,’ ‘Much Music Video Awards,’ and ‘MTV Video Music Awards.’ He has also won 2 ‘BET Hip Hop Awards’ and 1 ‘BET Award’.
