Since 2011, at least 11 sets of remains have been discovered along Ocean Parkway. He lives in a peaceful Massapequa Park neighborhood, but on Friday morning the police, the media, and curious onlookers descended upon his modest red house.

Heuermann revealed to his presenter during the interview with Bonjour Realty that he started working in Manhattan in 1987. He responded to the interviewer’s question on what sort of tool he would be by describing his father as an aeronautical engineer who worked on satellite construction while also dabbling in furniture making, which he currently does in the same workshop as his father.

Heuermann claimed that he would serve as a carpenter’s hammer. “It is persuasive enough,” he said, raising his eyebrows and tilting his head. “When I need to persuade something, not someone, something, and it always yields excellent results. And at the end of the project, whatever piece of furniture or what I’m working on, it always helps it come out beautifully.”

The interviewer questioned if he would be that kind of hammer for his work.

“Sometimes I have to be the heavy framing hammer, other times I am the lightweight hammer just to nudge things along,” Heuermann said.
