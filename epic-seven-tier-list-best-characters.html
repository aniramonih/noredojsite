Epic Seven Tier List - All Characters Ranked

Posted by Tandra Barner on Friday, August 9, 2024

Epic Seven has plenty of characters to choose from, and here's the tier list ranking all characters from best to worst.

First Published April 21, 2023, 18:43

Epic Seven is one of the most successful RPG titles developed by Super Creative Studios and published by Smilegate. Similar to other games in its genre, Epic Seven boasts a massive collection of characters, with each individual having a unique skill set, abilities, and styles.

Having the best heroes in your Epic Seven team will enable you to combat the toughest enemies. However, choosing the best characters is a bit tricky task, especially when there are over 200 characters to choose from. 

However, to save you from this time-consuming process, we have prepared the Epic Seven tier list ranking all the characters from best to worst.

Epic Seven Tier List 

The characters in the tier list are listed under five levels explained as follows:

  • S-Tier: The best characters in the game, taking into account the current metagame.

  • A-Tier: Characters that are remarkable and very good, useful for whatever situation you face.

  • B-Tier: Good characters who can balance our team if we don't have the best.

  • C-Tier: Not-so-bad characters, but they should be replaced by better ones as soon as you get the chance to do so.

  • D-Tier: The ones you must avoid, only for emergency use or if you don't have a character to fill in a specific role.

So, there you have it, the best-Epic Seven characters ranked. We’ll be updating this tier list whenever new characters are released, so make sure you bookmark this page and come back later.
