Blount County, Tennessee, is gripped with concern after Florence “BJ” Neal reported that her daughter, Madison Nicole Neal, is missing.

The 15-year-old was last seen at their family home on the morning of March 24 and has not been seen or heard from since.

BJ, a school bus driver, reported leaving the House at around 6 am on March 24 to drive her bus route.

At that time, Madison was sound asleep in her bed. However, when BJ returned home at approximately 9 am, she was shocked to find that her daughter had disappeared.

Chief Deputy John Argo said Maddie’s disappearance occurred three hours after BJ worked.

He also said that Police are looking for a white Ford “dually” pickup truck with four doors that was seen in the area, but there haven’t gotten any clues so far.

BJ informed the officials that she had kept Madison out of school on Friday so they could visit a family member in the hospital in Knoxville.
