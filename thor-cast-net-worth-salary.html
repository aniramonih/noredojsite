Natalie Portman Net Worth

Natalie Portman, known for her role as Thor’s love interest in the franchise, has a net worth of $90 million. Additionally, she has received critical acclaim for her performances and has won several awards, including an Academy Award for Best Actress. Portman’s net worth is further boosted by her profitable endorsement deals. She has collaborated with luxury brands such as Dior and has been the face of their campaigns. In addition to her acting income, Portman has diversified her career by establishing her own production company, Handsomecharlie Films. Through this venture, she has produced and directed films, showcasing her talents behind the camera.
