UGAs Devin Willock and staffer Chandler Leroy Died in a Car Crash

Posted by Billy Koelling on Thursday, August 8, 2024

According to recent news, both Devin Willock and staffer Chandler Leroy are dead after being a victim of an accident

Hours after the football team celebrated its National Championship with a parade on January 15, 2023,

Devin Willock and a recruiting assistant Chandler LeCroy were killed in a crash, the club reported.

The single-car crash also wounded two other people, both football players, according to the school.

According to a press release sent to Heavy by the Athens-Clarke County Police Department, LeCroy, 24, was operating a 2021 Ford Expedition in the 900 block of Barnett Shoals Road at approximately 2:45 a.m. when the SUV veered off the road and collided with “two power poles and several trees.” Police claimed LeCroy was operating the Expedition in the outside lane while traveling south.

Our heartfelt condolences go out to the deceased’s family and friends, who have been struggling with the loss of such an intelligent and compassionate individual.
