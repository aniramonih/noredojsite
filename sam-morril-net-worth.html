Sam Morril Full Overview and Wiki

Early Life and Rise to Fame

Born in the late 1980s in Chelsea, Massachusetts, his life began with an artistic flair, being the son of painter Marilyn Greenberg and stepson of Mark Charles Morril, a publishing industry attorney.

His ethnicity, Ashkenazi Jewish, has subtly influenced his comedic style. A New York City upbringing and education at the prestigious Browning School followed by Tulane University, set the stage for Morril’s diverse exposure and eventual inclination towards comedy.

Career Breakthroughs

Morril’s career journey is a fascinating narrative of continuous ascent. Named one of Comedy Central’s Comics to Watch in 2011, Morril’s trajectory in the comedy world was set.
