Pregnant Teen &amp; Boyfriend Found Dead in Car Days after She Was Due at Hospital

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

A pregnant teenage girl and her boyfriend were found dead in a car days after she was scheduled to go to a hospital to induce labor and after the girl's family organized a search for them on Christmas night. According to the heartbroken teen girl's mom, her late daughter was "so, so happy because she was going to be a mommy." The case has left police officials perplexed.

Savannah Soto and the information concerning when she was last seen posted on December 27, 2023 | Source: YouTube/KENS 5: Your San Antonio News Source

In tragic news, a pregnant 18-year-old named Savannah Soto was found dead in a car on Tuesday after a search that spanned two days. Her boyfriend, Matthew Guerra, was also found deceased in the same vehicle.

Savannah Soto and Matthew Guerra posing for a picture posted on December 27, 2023 | Source: YouTube/KENS 5: Your San Antonio News Source

The couple were found after Soto was set to go to a hospital to induce labor on Saturday night as, according to a news outlet, she was a week past her due date.

Their deaths come after police officials at the Leon Valley Police Department had labeled them as missing persons and had begun looking into leads in their investigation to attempt to find them.

Savannah Soto posing for a selfie posted on December 26, 2023 | Source: Facebook/Joanie Sofia

Police officials working on the case have been left at a loss. On this, San Antonio Police Chief William McManus stated, "It's a very, very perplexing crime scene. Detectives are looking at this as a possible murder, but we're not sure. We can't say for sure what we have."

Savannah Soto sitting down for a picture posted on December 26, 2023 | Source: Joanie Sofia

Guerra and Soto's bodies were discovered after an unnamed source alerted the family that the car had been found in the parking lot of an apartment complex in San Antonio. The family then alerted the police, who traveled to the crime scene and believed that the bodies had most likely been there for three or four days.

Police officials outside the apartment complex where Savannah Soto and Matthew Guerra were found posted on December 27, 2023 | Source: YouTube/KENS 5: Your San Antonio News Source

Naturally, the late youngster's family is devastated. Soto's grandmother, Rachel, expressed her and her family's desire for answers, and the teen's heartbroken mom, Gloria Cordova, devastatingly divulged, "Savannah was so, so happy because she was going to be a mommy. It breaks my heart."

Rachel Soto talking about her missing granddaughter posted on December 27, 2023 | Source: YouTube/KVUE

According to Cordova, her daughter was so excited about her baby that she had already finished creating the nursery and was looking forward to giving birth with her mom by her side. Cordova said the last time she had seen Soto was on Friday at her apartment.

Savannah Soto posing for a picture posted on December 26, 2023 | Source: Facebook/Joanie Sofia

After failing to hear from Soto hours before she was supposed to show up for her labor induction, Cordova decided to go check on her, but she wasn't home. She then went to the hospital, and there was still no sign of her teenage daughter. The panicked mom then filed a report with the police, who then issued a CLEAR (Coordinated Law Enforcement Adult Rescue) Alert.

Rachel Soto crying about the tragic news concerning her granddaughter posted on December 27, 2023 | Source: YouTube/KENS 5: Your San Antonio News Source

On Christmas Eve, Cordova shared the news about her daughter and a picture of her on social media and asked for help from the public. Her exact statement read, "My daughter is missing...If you see her please reach out to me and lmk...I am worried about her safety."

After this post, Soto's family and friends gathered where she was last seen and organized their own search for her and Guerra on Christmas night where they drove around the area looking for them.

Savannah Soto posing for a mirror selfie posted on December 27, 2023 | Source: YouTube/KENS 5: Your San Antonio News Source

As the teen girl and Guerra's families mourn their significant losses, many continue to send their condolences to the bereaved loved ones.
