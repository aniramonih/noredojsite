The Aladdin cast Naomi Scott is married to Jordan Spence.

Posted by Patria Henriques on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

If you are a fan of the superhero film Power Rangers, then Kimberly Ann Hart, aka Pink Ranger, might not be a new name for you. Pink Ranger is one of the leading characters portrayed by American actress Naomi Scott. 

The English entertainment personality was born Naomi Grace Scott on May 6, 1993, in Hounslow, London, England. Her birth sign is Taurus. She is the only daughter of an Indian Gujrati descent mother, Usha Joshi, and English father, Christopher Scott.

Naomi has a brother named Joshua Scott. Regarding education, Naomi graduated from Davenant Foundation School located in Loughton, Essex. 

Celebrity Husband: Jordan Spence

The 27-years-old actress Naomi is also gaining popularity as the wife of an English sports personality Jordan James Spence, aka Jordan Spence. The 5-feet-11-inch tall Noami's beloved husband, Jordan, is a football player.

Spence plays for Dutch Association Football Club ADo Den Haag. He previously played for the football clubs like West Ham United, Ipswich Town, Milton Keynes Dons, Leyton Orient, Scunthorpe United F.C, and many others.

Married Since 2014

The Power Ranger's actress Naomi first met Jordan at the age of 16 in 2009, in a church situated in Redbridge, UK. After the first encounter, the duo shared a good bond and soon start their romantic affair.

Following four-year-long dating, Scott and Spence decided to take their relationship to the next level. They tied the wedding knot on June 7, 2014. The couple exchanged the wedding vows in front of their family members, friends, and relatives.

For the big day, the bride Naomi wore an embroidered strapless wedding gown designed by Mira Zwillinger. On the other hand, the groom, Jordan, dashes the wedding look wearing a blue tuxedo, blue waistcoat,  a white shirt, and a light blue tie.

Since the wedding day, Naomi and Jordan are sharing a healthy bond as a perfect married partner. As of 2020, the husband-wife duo is celebrating their sixth marriage anniversary blissfully. The married partner also attends public events together.

Acting Credits

The 5-feet-4-inch (163 cm) tall Naomi started her acting career in 2008. Her first leading role was as Megan in the comedy series Life Bites (2008-2009).

Afterward, in 2011, Scott got a chance to play the main role in the science-fiction drama series Terra Nova as Maddy Shannon. Similarly, she played Mohini Banjaree in the teen musical series Lemonade Mouth.

The Teen Choice Awards-winning actress Naomi is also gaining wide media popularity for appearing as Princess Jasmine in the animated film Aladdin. Her other movie and series credit includes The 33, Charlie's Angels, Lewis, The Martian, and By Any Means. 

For the information, Naomi is playing in an upcoming American science fiction comedy film, Distant.
