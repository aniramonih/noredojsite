Cell to Singularity Evolution Never Ends Secret Achievements Guide

Posted by Kelle Repass on Wednesday, August 21, 2024

How to get all secret achievements.

How to Obtain All Secret Achievements

Achievements

  • 1st achievement – is to unlock all sharks. Just come into the game daily within 6 weeks. One shark per week.

  • 2nd Achievement – click on Archie, thyroanosaur in tuxedo (in the Mesozoic Valley).

  • 3rd Achievement – find in the Chimpanzee Forest and click on it several times.

  • 4th Achievement – find Bust Darwin. Find people in Savannah (you can find lion, look at the sea). lion put on the left border the screen, the center will be chimpanzee, and on the right edge of the screen of Darwin on the island.

  • 5th Achievement – find the fossil bones of Archie. Choose lion. Make so that people stood at the left side of the screen, the right side of the screen will be bones.

  • 6th Achievement – find on a paradise bird. Choose all the same people. Look at chimpanzees. Once in 15 seconds between people and chimpanzees, on the background of the leaves, the paradise bird flies (brown, with a blue head) or look for the island with a bust of Darwin.

  • 7th Achievement – find on Pink Dolphin. Choose all the same people. Look at chimpanzees. In the left of the river that flows into the jungle. Dolphin jumps out of the water between the tree and the mountain.

  • 8th achievement – is to reach the singularity in 5 minutes.
  • 9th achievement – is to reach the singularity in 90 seconds. Do not have time? Use the clicker.
