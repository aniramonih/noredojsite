Ivan Cornejo Wiki

Ivan Cornejo, born in 2004 in Riverside, California, is an American regional Mexican music singer-songwriter. His passion for music was sparked by a breakup he experienced in middle school, which became a source of inspiration for his songwriting. He taught himself to play the guitar by watching YouTube tutorials, starting with Ritchie Valens' "La Bamba."

Cornejo's debut album, Alma Vacia, achieved considerable success. It reached number two on the US Billboard Regional Mexican Albums chart and number seven on the Top Latin Albums chart. The album sold 7,000 album-equivalent units, peaked at number 156 on the Billboard 200 chart, and remained in the top ten of the Regional Mexican Albums chart for 35 weeks. His songwriting skills also led him to top the Billboard Latin Songwriters chart in October 2021. Alma Vacia was also recognized as one of the best albums of 2021 by The New York Times.

One of Cornejo's singles, "Está Dañada," went viral on TikTok in September 2021 and even charted on the Billboard Hot 100, reaching number 61. This achievement made him the second regional Mexican artist to chart on the Hot 100. Billboard magazine acknowledged Cornejo's rising success by featuring him in their Artist on the Rise column in October 2021.

His second studio album, Dañado, debuted and peaked at number one on the Regional Mexican Albums chart in June 2022. It sold 8,000 album-equivalent units and accumulated 11.9 million on-demand streams. The album also reached number four on the Top Latin Albums chart and number 28 on the Billboard 200 chart. Cornejo's music draws influence from artists like T3R Elemento and Grupo Los De La O. His guitar solos often incorporate alternative rock elements, inspired by the genre his brother and mother listened to while he was growing up.

In recognition of his talent, Ivan Cornejo has been nominated for the New Regional Mexican Artist category at the 2022 Premios Juventud. His unique blend of regional Mexican music and alternative rock influences continues to captivate audiences, establishing him as a rising star in the industry.
