Boris Kodjoe Responds To Haters After Wife Nicole Ari Parker Says She Misses The Single Life

Posted by Kelle Repass on Friday, August 9, 2024

When you’ve been together as long as Boris Kodjoe and Nicole Ari Parker have been, there are things you start to reminisce about when the newness of the relationship was still there. While speaking about their union via Instagram Live, Nicole admitted that she misses the single life.

However, it’s not the way that it sounds. The actress loves her husband and doesn’t mean that she misses being single — she misses the little things that couples do when they first start dating.

‘Sometime in the last year or so I thought, you know, I wish that he was my boyfriend again. I don’t necessarily want to be single, but I miss the single life. I miss the text messages for no reason. I miss the coming up behind me when I’m scrambling eggs and just kissing me on the back of the neck.’

She explained wanting the romantics to be put back into their longstanding marriage.

‘I personally believe that if you’re in it for the long haul, that freshness and newness is still possible if you talk about it. Be like, ‘Look, I know we talk about date night. Let’s go have a date night. I know we talk about kissing and texting for no reason, but the last time you text me was over like the cabinet guy.’
