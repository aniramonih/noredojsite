Mickie James Defeats Jordynne Grace, Wins Knockouts World Championship At IMPACT Hard To Kill

Posted by Kelle Repass on Thursday, August 22, 2024

The Last Rodeo has reached a triumphant conclusion.

In the main event of IMPACT Hard To Kill, defeated Jordynne Grace to win the IMPACT Knockouts World Championship. James’ career was on the line, as per the stipulation of The Last Rodeo; she previously announced that she would retire after her next loss. Since then, James has remained undefeated, and she earned a shot against Grace, who won the title at IMPACT Bound For Glory 2022. In the end, despite a valiant effort from Grace, James emerged victorious.
