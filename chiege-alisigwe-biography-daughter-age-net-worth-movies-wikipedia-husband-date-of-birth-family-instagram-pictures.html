Biography

Chiege Alisigwe C. (born November 21, 1975) is a gifted Nigerian actress, model, on-air personality and businesswoman.

She became renowned due to her role in the Nollywood blockbuster movie; My Love.

Since she has made her debut in the industry, she has been able to feature in over 200 Nollywood movies, making her one of the experienced actresses in the industry.

Chiege Alisigwe had more than twenty decades of experience in the industry before she stopped acting and focused on her NGO project. She is one of the most awarded actresses in the country.

Early Life

Chiege Alisigwe was born in Orlu, Orlu Local government of Imo State.

She is the lastborn of a family of eleven children.

She was born and raised in the ancient town of Orlu. She was able to have her primary and secondary education in Enugu.

When she was young, Chiege Alisigwe was athletically agile, which enabled her to participate in many sporting activities, especially running.

She was also a member of the dramatic society during her secondary school days.

She received the full support of her family when she intended to venture into Nollywood, and she usually appreciates her strict father’s efforts that make things easier for her then. 

Education

Chiege Alisigwe had had primary and secondary education in Queens School, Enugu before proceeding to Federal polytechnic, Oko, where she obtained a Diploma degree in fine and applied arts.

After studying in Oko, she moved to the Institute of Management and Technology (IMT) in Enugu, where she obtained her Higher national diploma in fine and applied arts.

Career

The talented actress, Chiege Alisigwe entered the Nollywood industry in 1998 when she acted in the blockbuster movie “My Love.”

Her role in the film gave her a lot of fame. Since she made her debut, the famous and highly demanded actress has featured in over 200 Nollywood movies.
