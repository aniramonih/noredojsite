What is Laura Antonelli's Net Worth?

Laura Antonelli is an Italian actress who has a net worth of $1 million. Laura Antonelli was born in Pula, Istria, Italy in November 1941. She is now retired but starred in 45 films from 1965 to 1991. She was a model and appeared in advertisements for Coca Cola in Italy.

She made her American film debut in Dr. Goldfoot and the Girl Bombs in 1966 but had a breakthrough in Malizia in 1973. She also starred in the movies 16 Year Olds, Pardon, Are You for or Against?, The Archangel, Venus in Furs, Detective Belli, Bali, Gradiva, A Man Called Sledge, The Married Couple of the Year Two, Without Apparent Motive, The Senator Likes Women, Dr. Popaul, How Funny Can Sex Be?, Malicious, Lovers and Other Relatives, Til Marriage Do Us Part, The Divine Nymph, The Innocent, Black Journal, Wifemistress, Tigers in Lipstick, I'm Getting a Yacht, Passion of Love, Chaste and Pure, The Venetian Woman, and The Trap. Antonelli won two Italian Golden Globes for Best Actress and Best Breakthrough Actress. She was convicted of possession and dealing cocaine but the conviction was overturned and the Italian court paid her $108,000 Euro.
