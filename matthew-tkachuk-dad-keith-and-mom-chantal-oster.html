Matthew Tkachuk Dad Keith and Mom Chantal Oster

Posted by Patria Henriques on Friday, August 30, 2024

Matthew Tkachuk dad Keith Tkachuk is a former ice hockey player who played 18 years in the NHL. Matthew grew up in Scottsdale Arizona.

Born in 1997, the 25-year-old winger is the substitute captain for the NHL side, Florida Panthers. 

Tkachuk is a very similar player to his father, Keith, with great physical attributes and who is not afraid to mix it up. Matthew has been named to the NHL All-Star team twice and won the Lady Byng Memorial Trophy in 2017.

Following Keith’s footsteps, he and his younger brother Brady Tkachuk have outstandingly emulated their old man’s achievements.

Matthew and Brady, the Ottawa Senators star, both played together for the Atlantic Division in the NHL All-Star game in South Florida in February 2023.

Matthew Tkachuk Parents

Matthew Tkachuk parents are Keith Tkachuk and Chantal Oster. The couple married on February 28, 1997 at Winnipeg City.

After wrapping up his college hockey career at Boston University Terriers in 1989, the Winnipeg Jets picked him in the 1990 NHL Draft. Keith spent four years playing for the Jets from 1992 to 1996.

While there, Keith met the love of his life, Chantal, from Winnipeg. One day Oster found herself at one of Keith’s games, where a captivating moment unfolded. Their gaze met, and in that instant, a deep connection formed within their hearts.

Keith and Chantal tied the knot in their early twenties and moved to Arizona in 1996. Soon after settling down, they wasted no time in starting a family.

Keith Tkachuk Played For Phoenix Coyotes

Keith played for four NHL franchises including the Phoenix Coyotes and St. Louis Blues.

Born in Melrose, Massachusetts, on January 29, 1967. Keith had a long career in the National League that spanned two decades, from 1992 to 2010.

Tkachuk made his NHL debut with the Jets in 1991 and promptly became one of the league’s top scorers. Later he was traded to the Phoenix Coyotes in 1996, where he helped the Coyotes reach the Stanley Cup playoffs in 1997, netting a career-best 52 goals.

The old Tkachuk is known for his physical play and his scoring ability. He was a 5-time All-Star and won the Hart Memorial Trophy as the NHL’s MVP in 1997. He was also on the squad of the US national hockey team that won gold at the 1996 World Cup of Hockey.

As an elite player in NHL history, Keith finished his NHL career with 508 goals and 1,016 points in 1,201 games. Furthermore, he is among the handful of American-born players to net 500 goals and the sixth American player to score 1,000 points in over 1200 league games.

Chantal Oster

Matthew Tkachuk mom Chantal Oster runs Covenant House, which supports and assists young individuals facing homelessness.

Oster was born and raised in Winnipeg, Canada, and earned her graduation in business degree from the University of Manitoba. Mrs Tkachuk was working at Safeway when she met her husband Keith.

In an interview with The Athletic, Chantal revealed that despite being a business graduate, her true passion lies in architecture. If given another opportunity, she would enthusiastically pursue a career in designing buildings.

Chantal’s love for architecture has been evident as she actively participated in designing their family’s new home in St. Louis, which was completed just a year ago.

Matthew Tkachuk Siblings

Matthew has two siblings Brady Tkachuk and Taryn Tkachuk. Matthew is the eldest of the trio and grew up with them in Scottsdale, Arizona.

The Tkachuk brothers are considered by many as the top pair of siblings in the NHL right now. They both play with a tough and aggressive approach, similar to their legendary pop Keith.

His mother, Chantal said they would watch hockey every night while Keith played. Every morning before school, Matthew and Bradey would wake up, and their first action would be to switch on the NHL Network.

Brady Tkachuk Is The Ottawa Senators Captain

Matthew Tkachuk brother Brady Tkachuk is a National Hockey League player for the Ottawa Senators.

Forward Brady was selected by the Senators as the 4th overall pick in the 2018 NHL Entry Draft. Before turning professional, Tkachuk played one season for the Boston University Terriers, earning All-Hockey East Rookie Team accolades.

On the other hand, Brady is engaged to her long-time girlfriend Emma Farinacci.

When the Tkachuk brothers teamed up for the 2023 NHL All-Star game, it marked the third time Brady, who leads the Senators, was chosen for the All-Star event. Meanwhile, Matthew, who joined the Panthers this season, received his second All-Star selection.

Keith, Chantal, and Taryn attended the game to witness one of the greatest moments in their family’s athletic history. 

Taryn Tkachuk Plays Field Hockey At Virginia

Matthew Tkachuk sister Taryn Tkachuk is a talented field hockey player at the University of Virginia. Taryn was born on November 1, 2002 in Arizona. 

Chantal believes the family’s youngest member, Taryn is the best athlete among his three children. They also call her LeBron because she wears the No. 23 jersey. 

Taryn attended Villa Duchesne school in St. Louis, where she implanted seeds of her athletic brilliance. She also played briefly for the Oak Hill School.

Throughout her four years of playing, Taryn consistently outshone her peers, leading all high school players in the state of Missouri in both total points and goals. The 20-year-old topped the state’s assist charts twice during her high school career.

One particular season stood out for Taryn and her team at Villa Duchesne. She displayed her immense talent by leading Missouri in scoring at the varsity level, amassing an impressive 31 goals, 32 assists, and 94 points, and winning the state championship.

Matthew Tkachuk Family Life Growing Up

Tkachuk grew up with his family in the Phoenix outskirts of Scottsdale, Arizona. 

After Keith transferred to the St. Louis Blues in 2001, the clan relocated to Chesterfield, Missouri where Matthew spent the rest of his early years.

The household has an athletic mindset imbued deeply from the start as Keith’s three brood, Matthew, Brady and Taryn, all developed competitive spirits from their childhood.

Some FAQs

Is Matthew Tkachuk related to Keith Tkachuk?

Yes, Matthew Tkachuk is related to Keith Tkachuk. Matthew is the son of a former NHL star.

Did Keith Tkachuk win a Stanley Cup?

No, Keith did not win a Stanley Cup during his 18-year long NHL career. Keith announced his retirement in 2010.

Is Matthew Tkachuk related to Walter Tkachuk?

Matthew Tkachuk does not have any relation to Walter Tkachuk. Walter Tkachuk is a retired German ice hockey player who played in the NHL for 14 seasons.

On a casual Friday evening at her family’s home, Taryn teamed up with Brady to take on Matthew in an intense bubble hockey game. The friendly rivalry and laughter filled the air as they battled it out, each displaying their unique skills and competitive nature.

Matthew had to leave the comfort of his home following the 2016 NHL draft as Calgary Flames picked him in the 6th round. His grandfather Don moved with him to Calgary as his temporary roommate.

Additionally, Matthew is not only related to Tom Fitzgerald, who is the general manager of the New Jersey Devils and used to play in the NHL, but he is also a cousin of Kevin Hayes, who currently plays as a forward for the Philadelphia Flyers.
