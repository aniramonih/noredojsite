John Klenke Obituary, Auburn AL, Former State Rep. Has Died

Posted by Tandra Barner on Sunday, August 25, 2024
0SHARESShareTweet

John Klenke Obituary, Death – Former Green Bay state legislator John Klenke passed away on October 10 at the age of 65, as confirmed by his obituary. Klenke’s political career included a tenure in the Wisconsin Assembly from 2011 to 2015. His successor in the 88th Assembly District, fellow Republican John Macco, expressed his deep admiration for Klenke and how he had been instrumental in encouraging him to embark on his own political journey over a decade ago. Macco also expressed his gratitude for Klenke’s unwavering friendship and dedicated service to their community.

Klenke was a distinguished alumnus of Green Bay Southwest High School, where he graduated in 1976. His professional background was rooted in his more than two-decade-long commitment to Ashwaubenon-based Schneider National. Throughout his life, Klenke left an indelible mark on the political landscape of Wisconsin, and his influence extended to future generations of public servants.

John Klenke’s passing marks the end of an era for the Green Bay community, where his dedication to public service and his role in shaping state policies will be remembered with deep respect and appreciation. His legacy lives on through the people he inspired, and his contributions will continue to influence the political discourse of the region for years to come.
