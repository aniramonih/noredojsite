Kendrick Lamar’s parents: Who are Kendrick Lamar’s parents?

Duckworth came into the world on June 17, 1987, in Compton, California.

His parents, Paula Oliver and Kenneth “Kenny” Duckworth, both had humble beginnings in the Chicago projects, where they crossed paths and kindled a romance.

However, Kendrick’s father, Kenny, had been involved with the Gangster Disciples, an African American street and prison gang.

Facing an ultimatum from his girlfriend, Paula Oliver, Kenny had to make a life-altering decision: either leave behind his gangster lifestyle in Chicago or risk losing her.

In 1984, Kenny chose to break away from his gang affiliations, and the couple left the state behind.

They embarked on a journey to Compton, California, armed with only $500.

Upon arriving in Compton, the couple initially resided in a hotel and worked various odd jobs to make ends meet, with Kenny at KFC and Paula at McDonald’s.

Those initial three years in Compton were marked by financial hardships.

Nevertheless, through diligent saving, they eventually secured an apartment, and it was there that their son, Lamar, came into the world.
