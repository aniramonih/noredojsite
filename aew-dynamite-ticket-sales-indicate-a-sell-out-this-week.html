AEW Dynamite Ticket Sales Indicate A Sell-Out This Week

Posted by Tandra Barner on Saturday, August 24, 2024

AEW Dynamite is coming to Indianapolis, IN on November 30th. Much like every week, this week’s show promises to provide an exciting night of action and is close to being sold out.

WrestleTix’s Patreon reported that out of the current seating capacity of 4,616, 3,429 tickets have been distributed. This means that only 1,187 tickets are yet to be sold. Given that the 3/4th of the tickets have been sold with two days to go, it is most likely that the show will be sold out or is close to selling out.

AEW Dynamite/Rampage Wed

Nov 30 • 7:00 PM Indiana Farmers Coliseum, Indianapolis, IN

Available Tickets => 1,187

Current Setup/Capacity => 4,616

Tickets Distributed => 3,429

Given the size of the venue, if AEW had only managed to sell 50% of the tickets, then the company might need to be concerned. Those sales indicate that AEW will do very well this week. Most venues sell about 1k tickets on the day of the show, or the few days leading up to the show. So, AEW should be able to sell out for this week.
