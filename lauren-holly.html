57 years old Lauren Holly Is Married Thrice: Details here

Posted by Patria Henriques on Thursday, August 29, 2024

American-Canadian actress Lauren Holly is known for her prominent role in NCIS as Jenny Shepard and Mary in Dumb and Dumber. Her professional life is crystal career to her fans. What about her personal life? 

Here know all the details of Lauren Holly. 

Lauren Holly's Early Life And Education

Lauren Holly was born on 28 October 1963 and is 57 years old as of 2021. She was born in Bristol, Pennsylvania, the United States, as Lauren Michael Holly to mother Michael Ann Holly and father, Grant Holly. 

Her father, Grant, is a professor of literature & screenwriter, and her mother is an art historian. 

She grew up in her hometown with her siblings: brother Nick Holly and Alexander Innes Holly. Her brother Alexander died in 1992 due to the fire incident. Her family established The A Fund in the memory of Alexander at Hobart and William Smith Colleges. 

Lauren Holly holds dual nationality, American-Canadian citizenship, and she belongs to a white ethnic background. 

Regarding her education, Lauren Holly went to Geneva High School. After she graduated from high school in 1981, Holly attended Sarah Lawrence College. From college, she got an undergraduate degree in English. 

Lauren Holly Is Married Thrice!!! Currently, Dating Someone? 

Yes, Lauren Holly is married. The 57 years old is married three times as of 2021. 

Firstly, she was married to her longtime boyfriend, Banny Quinn. They are college friends. After dating for a few years, they exchanged their marital vows in 1991 in a lavish wedding ceremony. They separated after a couple of years in 1993 but did not disclose the reason for their divorce. 

A year later, she met with her second husband, Jim Carrey. They fell for each other while working together in the movie Dumb and Dumber. After dating for three years, Carrey and Holly got married on 23 September 1996, but they separated within a year on 29 July 1997. 

Her third husband is an investment banker, Francis Greco. They walked down the aisle on 10 March 2001. She got her Canadian citizenship through her third spouse in 2008. 

The couple adopted three children: all sons Alexander Holly-Greco, Henry Holly-Greco, and George Holly-Greco. After 17 years of married life, they divorced with mutual understanding in 2014. 

After leading three unsuccessful marriages, the mother of three is in a romantic relationship with Jerome Ruffin.

Lauren Holly's Career And Net Worth

Lauren Holly started her professional career as an actress in her early 20s. Her first on-screen role was in the TV series Hill Street Blues as Carla Walicki. 

In 1992, she landed a major role in the TV series Picket Fences as Maxine Stewart. Eventually, she appeared in popular movies and TV shows like Dumb and Dumber, Dragon: The Bruce Lee Story, What Woman Want, Tiny Pretty Things, Motive, NCIS: Naval Criminal Investigative Service, Christmas Catch, Lucifer, and many others. 

As of early 2021, Holly has around 102 acting credits. She has won four awards including the 2016 Canadian Filmmaker's award, 1995 MTV Movie Award, and other two. In total, she has been nominated in 14 awards. 

What is Lauren Holly Net Worth? 

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Lauren Holly is estimated to have a net worth of $14 million as of 2021. 

She earns her net worth from her thriving professional career.
