James Murphy's About

[✎]

James Murphy was born on February 4, 1970 (age 53) in Princeton Junction, New Jersey, United States. He is a Celebrity Music Producer. Singer-songwriter and producer best known for his electronic/dance project LCD Soundsystem. He released the self-titled album LCD Soundsystem in 2005 and two more albums, Sound of Silver in 2007 and This is Happening in 2010, before beginning to focus solely on producing other artists. According to numerology, James Murphy's Life Path Number is 5. His genres are Dance-punk,electronica,post-punk revival,alternative dance,indie rock. His the best movies are Shut Up and Play the Hits (2012), Greenberg (2010), While We're Young (2014), The Comedy (2012) and Little Duck. His successful albums are Greenberg, This Is Happening, American Dream. He is a member of music group LCD Soundsystem.
