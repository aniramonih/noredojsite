Jessica Simpson Wears Sexy Swimsuit on Birthday Vacation: Pics

Posted by Patria Henriques on Tuesday, August 13, 2024

Thirty-five never looked so good! Jessica Simpson is spending her birthday in St. Bart's for a two-week bash with her family and friends. And on Saturday, July 11, the birthday gal shared a series of photos from her sunny getaway. 

First she posted a black and white photo of herself wearing a Native American headdress with the caption, "35."

Then the beach-ready babe posed with pals Lauren Harrison and Stephenie Pearson while wearing a black swimsuit with a black netted cover-up. 

"I love these vacation babes @laurenz422 @stepheniepearson," Simpson captioned the pic. 

The mother of two is enjoying some much-needed rest and relaxation with her hubby, Eric Johnson, and kiddos, Maxwell, 3, and Ace, 2. But just because it's a family outing doesn't mean the fashion designer isn't having a little fun of her own. 

She also shared a picture with her stylist friend Nicole Chavez hitting the dance floor, writing, "My colorful spiritual guide @nicolechavezstyle #beautifulmoment."
