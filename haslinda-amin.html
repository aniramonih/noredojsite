Is Bloomberg TV News Anchor, Haslinda Amin Married? Who Is Her Husband?

Posted by Kelle Repass on Sunday, August 11, 2024

Haslinda Amin is a Singaporean journalist, who covers current affairs specializing in business, economy, and politics. She reported on financial and political scandals, including the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation forums, the bloody Anti-government clashes in Thailand, irregular supply of products in Pakistan, and 44th U.S. President, Barack Obama's first visit to Asia.

Furthermore, Amin is the executive producer and host of the longest-running program, High Flyers on Bloomberg TV. On her talk show, she has taken high-profile interviews with the 46th U.S. president, Joe Biden; President of Indonesia, Joko Widodo, and late Prime Minister of Singapore, Lee Kuan Yew (who is also known as the country's founding father).

Early Life of High Flyers Host, Haslinda Amin 

Voyager actress, Haslinda was born and raised in a modest family in Singapore. During her high school days, she had a desire to play netball for her country's national female netball team. However, she faced a bad knee injury that ended her dream to become a professional netball player. She later recovered from the injury after taking good rest and exercising some crunches with the help of her fellow teammate, Karl.

After high school graduation, Haslinda went to attend the National University of Singapore for higher studies. She later graduated with a Bachelor of Arts (B.A.) in Political Science and Government. During her tenure, she played netball for Singapore Youth Squad and Varsity Team. 

Some Career Highlights of Haslinda Amin

Asian TV Award-nominated news anchor, Haslinda Amin began her career as a markets reporter, broadcasting live from the Singapore trading floor for Bloomberg LP, a media company based in Manhattan, New York City in 2002. After serving three years as a reporter, Bloomberg promoted her as the Chief Asia Correspondent in 2006.

Amin then anchored a daily 2-hour news segment, The Trade from 2009 to 2011. During the live show, she reports the stoke market analysis and price determination upon the commentary of traders, stockbrokers. 

Amin interviewed plenty of high-profile public figures, including Australian Prime Minister, Julia Gillard; successful business tycoons, Wolfgang Puck, and Jimmy Choo on her talk show, High Flyers. Furthermore, she exclusively broadcast the current affairs as the chief international correspondent for Bloomberg since 2011.

How is Haslinda Amin's Married Life Going with Phill Amin?

UN Women Singapore board member, Haslinda Amin is sharing her quality moments with her husband, Phill Amin in a blissful marital union for many years. The duo married in a low-key wedding ceremony, where their family members, close relatives, and friends attended.

Haslinda loves to share pictures of her traveling memories with her husband, Phil on social media. Moreover, they went to Rome, Italy to spend their summer holidays and posted a glimpse of their photos via Instagram on 9th March 2019.   

On the other hand, Haslinda wished her spouse, Phill on his birthday by sharing a picture of celebrating his born day on 7th February 2021 through an Instagram post.
