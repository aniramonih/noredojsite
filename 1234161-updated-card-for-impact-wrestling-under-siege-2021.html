Updated Card For IMPACT Wrestling Under Siege 2021

Posted by Billy Koelling on Monday, August 26, 2024

IMPACT Wrestling Under Siege is filling out and will feature the former Big Cass, Kenny Omega, and also a match to determine his next challenger for the IMPACT World Championship.

On tonight’s episode of IMPACT Wrestling, the rest of the field for the six-way scramble was determined as Chris Sabin, Moose, and Trey Miguel have all qualified and will join Sami Callihan, Matt Cardona, and Chris Bey in the fight to determine the next challenger to Kenny Omega’s IMPACT Title.

The full lineup for the 6 Way #1 Contenders Match at #UnderSiege on May 15th is set! @SuperChrisSabin vs. @DashingChrisBey vs. @TheSamiCallihan vs. @TheMattCardona vs. @TheTreyMiguel vs. @TheMooseNation! pic.twitter.com/wkAI2oqfpO

Also, two singles matches were made official. First, W. Morrissey, the former Big Cass in WWE, has accepted the challenge of Willie Mack to meet in singles action. On top of that, Brian Myers, who has run afoul of Decay, will face Black Taurus on Saturday, May 15.

You can see the official card below:

  • Kenny Omega & The Good Brothers (Doc Gallows & Karl Anderson) vs. Eddie Edwards & FinJuice (David Finlay & Juice Robinson)
  • Six-Man Number One Contenders Match: Chris Bey vs. Matt Cardona vs. Sami Callihan vs. Chris Sabin vs. Trey Miguel vs. Moose
  • Knockouts Tag Team Championship Match: Jordynne Grace & Rachael Ellering (c) vs. Fire ‘N Flava (Tasha Steelz & Kiera Hogan)
  • Willie Mack vs. W. Morrissey
  • Black Taurus vs. Brian Myers
