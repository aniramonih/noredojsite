Madelyn Cline Family Are Parents Pam Cline and Mark Cline

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, August 4, 2024

Madelyn Cline family members Pam and Mark are very far from the showbiz industry. Pam is a real estate agent and Mark works for Charleston Water System. 

Cline's onscreen character Sarah has well-to-do parents from the wealthy side of The Cut called the Kooks. However, her real-life parents Pam and Mark also do not fall behind their on-screen counterparts.

However, her childhood and parents are very far from Sarah's villainous super-rich father and stepmother. Madelyn grew up as an only child of her father and mother as opposed to her onscreen alter ego growing up with a troubled brother and supportive younger sister. 

Her Father Mark Cline

Madelyn Cline father Mark Cline is an engineer. 

According to his LinkedIn profile, Mark works at Charleston Water System as the Cheif Executive Officer (CEO). According to Charleston Water System, Mark joined the company in 1984 in a lower position and became the CEO of the organization in 2007.

He also served as the Vice President of the American Water Works Association for a year between June 2012 and June 2013. Mark is also a member of the Water Environment Federation and the American Society of Civil Engineers. 

Cline graduated from North Carolina State University in 1981 with a Bachelor's in Engineering degree in Civil Engineering. He also has a professional engineering license and is also a certified water treatment plant operator. 

Her Mother Pam Cline

Madelyn Cline mom Pam Cline is a real estate agent based in South Carolina.

Pam traveled with Madelyn when she was just a 5th grader back in 2008 to New York for eight weeks to shoot multiple commercials and photos for different companies, according to The Post and Courier. 

She works as a real estate agent locally around  Goose Creek and Charleston in South Carolina. However, while she is not selling dream homes to her clients, Pam is seen on the back of Quater Horse doing Barell races. 

Unlike her husband, Pam has kept both her professional and personal life away from the media and the internet. She does not share her only daughter's spotlight and barely speaks with the media.
