Early Life

She hails from a family of actors as her mother who gave birth to her in California in 1969 was actress Nacy Dow, and the father was actor John Aniston. She has two siblings John and Alex.

She went to Walford School, then joined Manhattan’s Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School of Music & Art and Performing Arts.

Currently, Jennifer Aniston is the wife of Justin Theroux whom she got married to in 2015 after meeting him in 2011. before she had been a wife of Brand Pitt for seven years before they separated in 2005 due to Pitt infidelity with Angelina Jolie, she also dated Daniel McDonald, Vince Vaughn, and John Mayer.

Age, Height, and Weight

Being born on 11 February 1969, Jennifer Aniston is 55 years old as of today’s date 20th July 2024. Her height is 1.64 m tall, and her weight is 58 kg.

Career

Aniston didn’t quite get into acting immediately but started as a bike messenger, telemarketer, and even worked as a waitress at some point in her life. Her debut in acting kicked off when she was given a role in her series Molloy in 1990, then went ahead to co-star in Ferris Buellers Day Off 1986, and Camp Cucamonga in 1990.

Her breakthrough into film career came when she was cast as Rachel Green in NBC,s sitcom Friends 1994-2004. She became a worldwide star, won a Golden Globe Award, and also became the highest-paid actress in the world earning $1 million per episode.

Other films she appeared on, Friends with Money 2006, between 2002 and 2012 are Good Girl 2002, Bruce Almighty 2003, Derailed 2005, The Break Up 2006, Marley & Me 2008, He’s Just Not That Into You (2009), The Bounty Hunter 2010, Just Go with It (2011), Horrible Bosses (2011), among others.

Since then she has appeared in a number of other films such as Wanderlust 2012 which grossed around $21 million, We’re the Millers (2013) grossing at $269 million, Cake (2014), Mother’s Day (2016), Storks 2016 grossing around $182 million, Office Christmas Party (2016) and The Yellow Birds 2018, and Dumplin 2018.

Awards & Achievements

Aniston has been honoured with many accolades and nominations as well. Some of the awards she has won are 1 Alliance of Women Film Journalists Award, 1 Golden Globe Award, 1 Hollywood Film Award, 2 MTV Movie + TV Award, 7 People’s Choice Awards, 6 Teen Choice Awards among others.
