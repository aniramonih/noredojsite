Tory Kittles Bio (Wiki)

Posted by Kelle Repass on Tuesday, August 13, 2024

Who is Tory Kittles?

Tory Kittles is an American actor, writer, plus director.

He is popular for his amazing acting in Malibu’s Most Wanted (2003), Get Rich or Die Tryin’ (2005), and Next (2007).

Recently, he played the character of Detective Marcus Dante in the TV series called The Equalizer.

Who are the birth facts of Tory Kittles?

His birthdate is 07 May 1975 and he was born in Lawtey, Florida, the US. The information about his mother along with his father is not disclosed.

As of 2023, his age is 47 years old.

Tory’s ancestry is All-American and has a brother named Omar. Regarding his academics, the data is still not revealed.

Tory Kittles- Professional Life, Career

Tory Kittles is a popular face in the entertainment world. His first movie was named Tigerland in 2000 where he played the role of Ryan.

He also did other movies titled Paparazzi in 2004, A Perfect Getaway in 2009, The Sapphires in 2012, American Heist in 2014, Dragged Across Concrete in 2018, Those Who Wish Me Dead in 2021, and more.

In addition, Tory appeared TV series like Sons of Anarchy as Laroy Wayne, True Detective as Detective Thomas Papania, Intruders as Gary Fischer, Colony as Broussard, etc.

The list of his other brief TV projects are,

  • The Catch in 2016 as Brian Brooks
  • CSI: Miami in 2012 as Delonte Cassell
  • Fear Clinic in 2009 as Jonte
  • House M.D. in 2007 as Derek Hoyt
  • The Real Whatever in 2000 as Marcus
