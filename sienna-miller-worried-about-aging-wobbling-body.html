Sienna Miller worried about aging, wobbling body

Posted by Billy Koelling on Thursday, August 15, 2024


Sienna Miller is trying desperately to prove herself to the sisterhood she feels has shunned her. She gave a recent interview to Style Magazine, and she mouthed off about her looks as seen though Hollywood’s strict standards. The 27-year-old claims that “things are wobbling” on her body, that she has wrinkles, that she worries about her aging. But at the end of the day, Sienna says “f–k it” and claims she can’t wait to “be seen as a woman”.

It seems like Sienna Miller is really trying to get in someone’s good graces, but it just feels like the condescending thin girl complaining about her weight to her friend with weight issues. Sienna’s like “No really, I worry about my appearance, too, I’m just like you!” as she faces down an angry mob of Balthazar Getty’s wife’s friends. Who are wielding pitchforks. Now Magazine has more:

Sienna Miller frets about losing her looks – but not enough to take drastic action.

‘Urgh, the pedestal thing stops me sleeping,’ she tells Style. ‘Recently I found my self thinking, “I’m getting wrinkles. And things are wobbling, I should go to the gym.”

‘Then I was like, f*** it, actually. It’s not where the focus should be right now. LA is a nightmare for that. Young girls in Hollywood are having pre-emptive Botox.’

‘I say, let people be young and then be old. I can’t wait to be seen as a woman. And I know I probably have to contribute to that with my behaviour.’

Sienna, 27, is dating “Brothers & Sisters” star Balthazar Getty 33.

From Now Magazine

Sienna is right about how younger women are going in for “procedures”, whether it’s actually plastic surgery or just Botox injections or fillers. It’s a disturbing trend, and one that I hope dies out. If Sienna is really worried about her appearance and aging and going “wobbly”, I have a suggestion. Heavy drinkers look worse and age faster than non-drinkers or moderate drinkers. Perhaps Sienna should just spend fewer nights drunkenly trolling for married men?

By the way, it was made official last week – according to multiple sources, Cate Blanchett is really going to take over the Maid Marian role in Ridley Scott’s Nottingham. This is the role that Sienna Miller tried to say she was “too thin” and “too young” to play against Russell Crowe’s Robin Hood.
