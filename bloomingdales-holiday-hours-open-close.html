Bloomingdale’s Holiday Hours Open & Close: Know When to Shop

As the holiday season approaches, it’s important to know when your favorite stores will be open and closed. Bloomingdale’s is a popular department store that offers a wide range of products. If you’re planning to do your holiday shopping at Bloomingdale’s, you’ll want to know when the store is open and closed. In this article, we’ll provide you with all the information you need about Bloomingdale’s holiday hours.

What Time Does Bloomingdale’s Close and Open?

Bloomingdale’s stores have different opening and closing times depending on their location. However, most stores open at 10:00 AM and close at 9:00 PM from Monday to Saturday. On Sundays, stores open at 11:00 AM and close at 7:00 PM.

Is Bloomingdale’s Open on Saturday and Sunday?

Yes, Bloomingdale’s is open on Saturdays and Sundays. As mentioned previously, stores typically open at 10:00 AM on Saturdays and close at 9:00 PM. On Sundays, stores open at 11:00 AM and close at 7:00 PM.

Bloomingdale’s Hours

Here’s a breakdown of Bloomingdale’s hours from Monday to Sunday:

  • Monday to Saturday: 10:00 AM to 9:00 PM
  • Sunday: 11:00 AM to 7:00 PM

Shopping Tips for Bloomingdale’s Holiday Hours

Shopping during the holiday season can be overwhelming, especially during peak hours. Here are some tips to make your shopping experience at Bloomingdale’s as smooth as possible:

  • Shop early in the morning or late in the evening when stores are less crowded.
  • Consider shopping online and taking advantage of free shipping and returns.
  • Make a list of what you need to buy and stick to it to avoid overspending.
  • Take advantage of Bloomingdale’s promotions and discounts to save money.
  • Check Bloomingdale’s website or social media pages for the latest information about holiday hours and promotions.
