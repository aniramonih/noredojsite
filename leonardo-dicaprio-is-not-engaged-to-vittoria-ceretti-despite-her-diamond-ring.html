Leonardo DiCaprio is not engaged to Vittoria Ceretti, despite her diamond ring

Posted by Patria Henriques on Sunday, August 11, 2024

Last spring/summer, Leonardo DiCaprio finally settled on one girlfriend, his first since his four-year relationship with Camila Morrone ended in 2022, when she sadly turned 25 and aged out of the Leo D Dating Experience. Leo’s new girlfriend turned out to be the Italian model Vittoria Ceretti, who had seemingly just left her husband two seconds before she started up with Leo. Leo and Vittoria were spotted together a lot last year, but it’s been several months since we’ve heard much about them or seen them out together. Then, this week, they had lunch in LA and paparazzi got some shots of them:

Leonardo DiCaprio, 49, and Vittoria Ceretti, 25, spark engagement rumors as she flaunts new ring https://t.co/shpvsmRUtT pic.twitter.com/SY1OEFNFKs

— Page Six (@PageSix) March 27, 2024

As you can see, there was a buzz about Vittoria’s ring. To be fair, it does not look like an engagement ring. To be fair, I’m not even sure her divorce has gone through either. But in any case, you can always trust that Leo’s team will ALWAYS swiftly deny any engagement rumor:

Leonardo DiCaprio and Vittoria Ceretti are not taking the next step in their relationship just yet.

“Leo and Vittoria are not engaged,” a source exclusively tells Us Weekly. “She’s wearing a ring she’s had for years.”

[From Us Weekly]

Yeah, I don’t believe they’re engaged either, nor do I believe that Vittoria was even trying to put that out there, unlike some of Leo’s previous girlfriends. Vittoria does seem different and she’s bringing a different energy to the Leo D Girlfriend Experience. I genuinely hope she eats him alive. Like, stomps on his heart and makes him cry. It will be good for him. Also: Vittoria is already 25! She turns 26 in June. I have a theory that Leo got clowned so hard in 2022, after his split from Camila, that he revised his age cutoff to 27/28.
