Delving Into the Relationship of Bethany England and Stephanie Williams: Relationship History

Posted by Billy Koelling on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

Bethany England is currently engaged to her longtime girlfriend, professional soccer player Stephanie Williams. The two reportedly started their relationship in 2018 and have been together since. Stephanie and Bethany have been a source of support for each other. The soccer player confirmed her engagement to her partner in February 2023. Bethany England, born on June 3, 1994, is an English professional soccer player who plays as a forward for FA WSL team Tottenham Hotspur. She began playing soccer at the age of six, initially with a boys’ squad, and was raised alongside two sisters.

At the age of 13, she joined Sheffield United’s girls’ squad, playing alongside her twin sister, Laura. She made her senior debut with Doncaster Belles on July 23, 2011. England later played for Chelsea in the 2016-17 season before joining Liverpool on loan in the 2017-18 season. After her time at Liverpool, she returned to Chelsea for a second spell in 2018 and remained with the team until January 4, 2023, when she was acquired by Tottenham for a record-breaking sum of £250,000 ($300,000). She has also represented the England national team at various levels, including U19, U23, and senior.

Stephanie Williams, Bethany England’s Partner: Relationship Timeline

Bethany and Stephanie commenced their relationship in September 2018 and have a strong bond. Stephanie, Bethany England’s partner, is also a soccer player. Their shared love for soccer may have played a significant role in the inception of their romance. Moreover, on February 3, 2023, Bethany announced their engagement on Instagram.

They are frequently seen vacationing together and share their pet Golden Retrievers, Buddy and Dilys. Stephanie Louise Williams, born on August 12, 1992, is a Welsh professional soccer player who currently plays for Oxford United WFC as a midfielder. Williams began her junior career with Cardiff City LFC before being promoted to the senior squad in 2008. After playing for Cardiff City until 2015, she moved on to Yeovil Town before joining Oxford United in 2017. She has also represented the Welsh national team, earning her first senior international call-up in 2015.

Her Notable Move to Spurs and Participation in the World Cup

Having been with Chelsea for seven years, England’s decision to join Spurs in January was a challenging one, considering Chelsea’s significance in her professional career. Additionally, her transfer to Spurs drew immediate criticism as it set a record for the highest transfer fee for a British player in the women’s game. Nevertheless, Spurs offered England what she desired: an opportunity to play and score.

Her exceptional performance for Spurs secured her a spot on the women’s national squad for the 2023 FIFA Women’s World Cup. England was beyond thrilled upon receiving the call from Wiegman confirming her inclusion in the 23-member World Cup team.

“I tried to play it nice and cool but I hung up the phone and screamed my house down,” she said. “The first thing I did was call my mother and my partner. I was overjoyed.”
