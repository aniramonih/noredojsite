Renowned Relationship Expert, Derrick Jaxn, Announces Divorce from Wife DaNaia a Year After Admit

Posted by Patria Henriques on Monday, August 5, 2024

Derrick Jaxn took to Instagram to share the news of his impending divorce from his wife, Da’Naia Jackson.

The announcement of their divorce comes shortly after Da’Naia Jackson defended their marriage amid criticism.

A year ago, Derrick, a renowned expert on relationships, publicly admitted to being unfaithful to his wife. In a video they did together, his wife stood by him, holding hands as they addressed the issue (read here).

Derrick shared the news of their divorce on Instagram on December 10.

He expressed, “Over the past several months, my family and I have undergone significant changes. Some of you have speculated, while others have offered support as we navigated this new phase privately, for ourselves and our children. Earlier this year, after extensive prayer, counseling, and thoughtful deliberation, we made the decision to separate and filed for divorce.”

 

The marriage between Derrick and Da’Naia lasted for four years.

The couple faced a considerable scandal when Derrick’s mistress, Candice De Medeiros, came forward in 2021, revealing that she had been involved with Derrick Jaxn on multiple occasions and had even been inside his family home. This revelation greatly impacted Derrick’s credibility as a relationship coach.
