Park Bom Nose Job: Surgery Update: Before And After Pictures

Posted by Billy Koelling on Friday, August 9, 2024

Park Bom nose job has become the talk of the town. But has the South Korean singer undergone the knife? Find it out below.

Park Bom is a prominent South Korean singer. The Seoul native is best known for being a former vocalist of the girl group 2NE1, active from 2009 to 2016.

She was the main vocalist of the group. During their heyday, 2NE1 became one of the most popular girl groups in South Korea.

Since beginning her musical career in 2006, Park has garnered insane fame and success. She ventured into the industry by featuring on singles created by labelmates Lexy, Masta Wu, and Big Bang.

Park Bom has released two solo singles, Don’t Cry and You and I. Her works have reached number one on the Gaon Digital Chart, Korea’s national music chart.

The 39-year-old is one of those K-pop idols who has always been drawn to the plastic surgery rumors. She has been the talk of the town due to her ever-changing appearance.

Did Park Bom Get A Nose Job? Surgery Update: Before And After Photos

Many people believe there have been significant changes in Park Bom’s appearance since she first ventured into the South Korean music industry.

According to people who have known the singer since the dawn, her nose used to be bulbous and large. Park’s nose is now slimmer and sharper.

Netizens believe that the changes in her facial features, particularly her nose, result from cosmetic procedures and are incomprehensible naturally.

However, there has been no official report or confirmation of the matter. In contrast, the singer’s agency once denied the plastic surgery rumors.

Park Bom Denied The Plastic Surgery Rumors In 2020

In June 2020, the gorgeous singer made a huge buzz following her appearance at the 56th Grand Bell Awards. The skilled vocalist graced the red carpet wearing a beautiful two-piece dress during the event.

But, netizens quickly pointed out the changes in her face, which ap to be swollen. They argued the former 2NE1 member looked outrageously different from the last time. Some people also said that Park Bom got plastic surgery.

Park Bom’s label, D-Nation, responded to the reports through an official statement and shut down all the allegations that she had undergone the knife.

According to the agency, Park had a hard time due to stress. So they assured her to be comfortable till the release of her new album. “This has led to weight gain and affected her face as well,” they added.

Another reporter from the company also denied the rumors before asking to look forward to her new look.

Moreover, in an interview with Star News, an insider stated that Park Bom was invited to perform at the Grand Bell Awards. Though she intended to take a break from public appearances until her comeback, she agreed to perform because she had never performed for an event like that.

The insider added that the singer had a hard time in 2019 as a solo artist which weakened her stamina. She had been regaining her stamina, resting and eating well.

Furthermore, Park Bom completed her first-ever “You & I” solo fan concert in Manila, Philippines, on 10 June.

The former 2NE1 vocalist appeared at the New Frontier Theater in her much-anticipated performance, demonstrating her live skills while singing songs like Spring (with Sandara Park), Don’t Cry, Lonely, and many others.
