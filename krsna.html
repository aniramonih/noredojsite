Krsna Facts

  • He was born in the Kashmiri Pandits family.
  • Growing up, he has spent some years in South London and also attended a school there.
  • At 14, he had started writing rap songs.
  • He started his singing career on MySpace in 2006, where he would record and upload his songs.
  • Krsna rose to fame with the release of his track Kaisa Mera Desh in 2010. From there onwards, he released a number of hit singles including Vyanjan, Vijay, and Last Night.
  • He had previously been performing under the moniker of Prozpekt. However, after he signed with Universal Music in 2013, he decided to change his stage name to “KR$NA”.
  • Krsna released his debut album titled Sellout in 2014. The entire album consisted of tracks that were released in English.
  • In 2017, Krsna signed an exclusive contract with DNH Artists.
  • In January 2021, he was featured alongside Royce da 5’9″ in the song, Crossroads.
  • In July 2021, KR$NA teamed up with Hi-Rez for a new single titled Playground.
  • In 2022, he was featured on Karan Aujla‘s song Ykwim.
  • In March 2023, KR$NA and Brodha-V won the Radio City Freedom Award for “Best Indie Collaboration”.
