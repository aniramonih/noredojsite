Did Calliope Mori Reveal Her Real Face?

Posted by Patria Henriques on Wednesday, August 7, 2024

Calliope Mori is a VTuber who has not revealed her face. Twitch viewers flood Calliope Mori with positive messages thanks to her ongoing success.

Calliope is an English-speaking female virtual YouTuber who was first introduced as a member of Hololive English.

According to her character bio, she considers herself the first apprentice of the Grim Reaper. Calliope decided to enter the virtual YouTuber career to harvest souls because modern medical care is causing a decrease in the reaping business.

She also claims that ascended souls of people evaporated because of the exciting interactions among virtual YouTubers also belongs to her as well. Still, She is a pure-hearted girl who deeply cares for her friends even though her character or avatar might seem dangerous.

Has Calliope Mori Done A Face Reveal?

Calliope Mori has not done a face reveal as of 2022.

Calliope's fans have asked her multiple times about her facial reveal; however, she keeps them, denying the opportunity to see how she looks in real life. 

Most VTubers, like Mori, tend to hide their face for personal reasons. Some even wait for a milestone in their career, like getting one million subscribers on their channel.

However, She has not shown any such reaction as her official YouTube channel has already crossed 2 million subscribers long ago.

Multiple videos online state that Mori has already shown her original personality. However, these are hoaxes to generate buzz and gather more view counts.

She is also active on Twitch with over 43.1k subscribers, where she frequently does different streams. But she never did the stream by facing the camera. Therefore, her fans would have to wait some more to see what their favorite virtual YouTuber looks like.

Mori made her debut stream on 12 September 2020. She was the first solo live-Mary-to member to make a debut.

Calliope Mori Is The Member Of Hololive English

Calliope Mori became part of the Hololive English virtual YouTubers group in 2020.

Cover Corporation had announced auditions for English-Speaking members to join the Japanese language Hololive virtual YouTubers group.

She got selected after different rounds of auditions. She started her training in June 2020 and had three months to prepare for her debut performance.

She was first introduced in a short promotional video, including her other team members. She made her first tweet on the same day after the official announcement was completed. 

Merchandise

Calliope Mori has released multiple songs after her debut, and they all have been made available on Spotify for viewers to listen to. 

Because of her popularity, she also has a merchandise website where different items related to her character and world are sold.

The website has also given access to buy the original CDs of her songs.

She also has another website available where different courses related to her grim reaper live, along with other accessories such as sweaters, stickers, wine glasses, and birthday posters, are also available for sale.

On October 22, she attained 500,000 YouTube subscribers, becoming the 16th member of hololive and the second person from the English branch to accomplish so.

She released a brand-new original song, Cursed Night, in November. She had a live karaoke webcast on December 14 to commemorate reaching 800,000 subscribers on her YouTube account.

She sang for her rap portions in the second original song by Tsunomaki Watame, Douten Hitsuji, on December 29.

She recently released a song called Wanted, Wasted ten days ago, which has already gained over three hundred thousand views.
