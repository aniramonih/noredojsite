Dhirendra Krishna Shastri Biography:- Dhirendra Krishna Shastri is the chief and the Maharaja of the Bageshwar Dham Sarkar. Bageshwar Dham Sarkar is a famous religious pilgrimage site in the Chhatarpur district of Madhya Pradesh.

He is known as Bageshwar Wale Maharaj. He is a very good narrator of the Bhagwat Mahapuran. He reads the mind of the people without being known anything from them. He also provides solutions to people’s problems. 

Dhirendra Krishna Shastri Biography, Wiki, Age, Family

Dhirendra Krishna Shastri was born on 4 July 1996 in Gada the village of Chhatarpur district. He spent his childhood in Gada. He belongs to a poor family.

He was born to Ram Karpal Garg (Father0 and Saroj Garg (Mother). His grandfather was a good scholar with Nirmohi Akhara named Bhagwan Das Garg.

His grandfather taught Dhirendra the study of Ramayana and Bhagwat Geeta. 

Since the age of 9, he was engaged in the service of Balaji. He followed the paths ordered by the Gurus and followed them like a real follower. By nationality, he is an Indian. 

Bageshwar Dham Bhoot Bhawan is located in Mahadev Gad which is called Chandel Carpet Sidhpith. This temple was built by the villagers in 1986. 

Dhirendra belongs to a Brahmin family. He is 26 years old as of 2023. His zodiac sign is Cancer. He is the devotee of Shree Balaji Maharaj.

He knows Hindi, English, Sanskrit, and Bundeli languages. He is famous for Divya Darbar. He follows the Hinduism religion.
