Miss Pooja Wiki: Salary, Married, Wedding, Spouse, Family

Miss Pooja (born 4 December 1980 as Gurinder Kaur Kainth) is an Indian professional singer and actress.Pooja has a Master's degree in music. She worked as a music teacher in Patel Public School in Rajpura, India before she embarked on her musical career.She is widely regarded as having revived Punjabi duet songs. She has become the largest-selling female bhangra artist in India and overseas. A partial reason for her success is the rapid increase in the number of songs that she continues to record. She has released duets with more than 70 different male singers. She joined Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) professing to work to check drug menace and female foeticide in Punjab. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
