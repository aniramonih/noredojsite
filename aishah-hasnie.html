American journalist Aishah Hasnie is well known for her outspoken personality and fearless reporting that mesmerizes millions of people. Hasnie was born on 5th October 1984 in Lahore, Pakistan, and raised in Bedford, Indiana, the United States, by Pakistani parents who immigrated from Pakistan. She grew up with her two siblings, including her younger sister, Afiah Hasnie, and one older brother. She holds an American nationality and belongs to Asian ethnicity. Actively Reporting in the News Field

The Indiana University Bloomington graduate, Hasnie started her journalism career while serving as an intern at WICS-TV news station situated, in Springfield, Illinois. During the internship, she also reported at WTHR-TV and GEO-TV. Similar to her, Jillian Mele pursuit success while serving as an intern at several news stations.

Aishah got her life-turning opportunity when the WANE-TV accepted her designation as a fill-in-anchor in October 2008. Four years later, she began working as an investigative reporter at WXIN-TV in 2011. Throughout her career, the Indiana Associated Press and the Society of Professional Journalists praised Hasnie for her outstanding reporting work.
