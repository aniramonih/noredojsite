Bhama (Actress) Height, Weight, Age, Husband, Biography, Family, Facts

Posted by Tandra Barner on Thursday, August 29, 2024

Bhama or “Rekhitha R. Kurup” (born May 23, 1989) is an Indian Actress from Kottayam, Kerala (India). She featured in the Malayalam, Tamil, Telugu and Kannada movies. In 2007, she made her film debut with Nivedyam (Malayalam film).

Bhama acted in several Malayalam movies like Cycle, One Way Ticket, Neelambari, College Days, 101 Weddings, D Company and Khilafat. Before being in a film, she casts in a TV show Thaali. She did a devotional music album.

Childhood, Parents & Siblings

Rekhitha R. Kurup was born to Rajendra Kurup & Shylaja Kurup on May 23, 1989. Moreover, her two elder sisters are Reshmitha and Renjitha Kurup.
