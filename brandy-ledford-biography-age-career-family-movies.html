BIOGRAPHY OF BRANDY LEDFORD

Brandy Ledford (Brandy Lee Ledford) is an American actress and model best known for being Penthouse Magazine’s “Pet of the Year” in 1992. She is also well known for her film roles such as Desiree in the sitcom family of television mockumentaries, “Modern Family”; Dawn Masterton on the action drama series ‘Baywatch Hawaii’; and as Doyle in the science fiction television series “Andromeda”.

Brandy was born on February 4, 1969 in Denver, Colorado. She also sometimes goes by the names of Jisel or Brandy Sanders. Her first known role was as Jisel in the 1991 adult film Penthouse: Fast Cars Fantasy Women, produced by a penthouse. The film features “naked bombs and hot rods.” That same year, she appeared as herself in the “Penthouse Pet of the Year Play-Off 1991” video.

Since then, Brandy has appeared in several other films and television series in various guest appearances in addition to being part of the main cast. She starred in several films including the action drama series, ‘Baywatch Hawaii’ as Dawn Masterton alongside star, David Hasselhoff. She appeared in about 15 episodes of the series from 1999 to 2000. This increased her recognition even more.

Recent works

Brandy then went on to appear in several other films and TV series, including First Target, Rat Race, Zebra Lounge, Faultline, Separate Ways, The Invisible Man (TV series), Smallville, Stargate SG-1, Whistler, as well as several others. . In 2005, she gained even greater recognition following her performance in the science fiction television series “Andromeda” as Doyle.

Brandy’s best-known portrayals from all of her appearances were in Andromeda, Baywatch Hawaii, The Invisible Man, and Whistler, for which she was nominated for two Best Actress awards, the Leo Awards, and the Gemini Awards. Lately, she appears in fewer and fewer scenarios over the years.

BRANDY LEDFORD AGE

She was born on February 4, 1969. She turns 50 in 2019.

BRANDY LEDFORD EX-HUSBANDS

Brandy has been married twice. At first, she married Damian Sanders, a snowboarder. They officially got married in the early 90s. At that time, she was appearing on Critical Condition and Snowboarders in Exile as herself. A few years later, they divorced. The reason why still remains unclear to the public

Then she married Martin Cummins, a Canadian actor best known for his work in “Dark Angel” (2001-02), “Riverdale” (2017) and “Poltergeist: The Legacy”, in 1998. The couple divorced in 2004 ., after about 6 years of marriage.

BRANDY LEDFORD CHILD

In her marriage to Cummins, she had one child. The identity of the child is unknown to the public.
