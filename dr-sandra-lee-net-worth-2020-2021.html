Early Life & Biography

On 20 December 1970, Sandra Lee was born in the Flushing section of Queens, New York City. Her parents are Chinese: her father is a retired dermatologist, while her mother is Malaysian Chinese. Also, her parents grew up in low-income families, each of 10 children. In 1969, they migrated to New York from China and later relocated to Southern California when Lee was five years old.

Lee schooled at UCLA as an undergraduate student, where she worked part-time as a medical assistant for an allergist in Downtown Los Angeles.

After her graduation from UCLA, she attended medical school at the Drexel University College of Medicine, where she graduated in 1998. Lee finished her dermatology placement at Southern Illinois University. Lee departed to San Diego to sharpen her skills in dermatological, laser, and cosmetic surgery after the end of her internship.

Presently, she lives in Upland, California, with her husband, Jeffrey Rebish, and works at Skin Physicians & Surgeons.

Career

Lee established her line of skincare products, SLMD Skincare Products, in 2017, which products included clarifying treatment with retinol, acne cleanser and lotion, and a daily moisturizer.
