Eric Church, Tim McGraw and Morgan Wallen Set To Headline the 2022 Faster Horses Lineup Celeb Se

Posted by Tandra Barner on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

Grab your best cowboy boots and saddles country fans, because the Faster Horses Festival is back for yet another year!

The ACM “Festival of the Year” announced that the three-day fest will return to Brooklyn, Michigan, on July 22, 23 and 24, 2022 with fun-filled events like rodeos and festivals along with a jam packed lineup of performers.

Headlined by none other than Tim McGraw, Eric Church and Morgan Wallen, the lineup also includes other country stars like Jake Owen, Brothers Osborne, Cole Swindell, Jordan Davis, Chris Janson, Ashley McBryde, Lauren Alaina, Michael Ray, Jameson Rodgers, Runaway June, Caitlyn Smith, Larry Fleet, Morgan Wade, Priscilla Block, King Calaway, Ray Fulcher, Restless Road, Tiera Kennedy, Dillon Carmichael, Ben Burgess, Ashley Cooke, Alana Springsteen, Angie K, Jackson Dean, Robyn Ottolini, Tyler Braden, Cooper Alan, DeeJay Silver with more to be announced.

The three-day festival will take place from July 22-24 in Brooklyn, MI and more details regarding non-musical activities for fans and campers to enjoy will be revealed closer to date.

Tickets for Faster Horses go on sale Friday, March 11 at 10AM ET on Faster Horses website.
