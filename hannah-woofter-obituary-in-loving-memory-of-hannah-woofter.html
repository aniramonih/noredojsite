Hannah Woofter Obituary

The city of Kansas City, Missouri lost one of its most beloved and respected residents with the passing of Hannah Woofter on October 31st, 2023 at the age of 82. Hannah was a lifelong inhabitant of the Kansas City metropolitan area and touched countless lives over her eight decades as a pillar of the community.

Born in Kansas City in 1941, Hannah was the youngest of three children and grew up in a modest home on the city’s east side. From a young age, Hannah’s compassion and care for others was apparent as she volunteered at local charities and befriended classmates who were bullied or marginalized. This spirit of selflessness and service shaped Hannah’s adulthood as she balanced raising a family with teaching elementary school and leading youth programs at her church.
