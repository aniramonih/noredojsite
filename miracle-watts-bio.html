Miracle Watts: A Closer Look at the Age, Height, Career, Net Worth and Love Life

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, August 27, 2024


Miracle Watts is a famous American Instagram star, model, social media influencer, and entrepreneur.

She was born on January 30, 1993, in Houston, Texas.

Her nationality is American while her ethnicity is Latina and African-American.

Watts spent her formative years in Houston and completed her high school diploma there.

She also attended college at some point but it is not known whether she completed her degree.

Since she was a child, Watts has been interested in fashion, beauty, and photography.

Before getting a shout-out on 2 On Thotful, she had made a name for herself as a professional twerker.

After the release 2 On Thotful, Watts made use of her rapidly growing popularity and set up her line of faux eyelashes, The Miracle Lash, and a line of faux hair, The Miracle Tresses.

Miracle Watts age

How tall is Miracle Watts?

Watts is 5 feet 4 inches tall, and she weighs approximately 61 Kg.

Miracle Watts career

Watts started as an exotic dancer in the popular Houston club Dreams.

She, along with Lira Mercer and Maliah Michel, was their biggest attraction.

This is how she met Drake, whose love for exotic dancers is well known.

He has rapped about going to Dreams with a suitcase.

Since becoming a social media influencer and an Instagram star, she has tried to maintain a level of consistency in her content.

She began her journey towards social media fame as a professional twerker.

In May 2014, OB O’Brien released his track ‘2 On Thotful’, featuring Drake.

Watts became a highly sought-after Instagram model and was represented by Facet Studios for a considerable period.

She came up with the idea of launching a line of faux eyelashes and a line of faux hair simultaneously.

However, it took her about four months to set up the line of eyelashes whereas a full year was spent on developing the line of hair.

How much is Miracle Watts worth?

Watts has a net worth of $1 million.

She has no doubt amassed a huge fortune with the bulk of her wealth coming from her professional entertainment career.

Miracle Watts and Tyler Lepley

Tyler Lepley and Watts are an American actor couple who met on the set of the Starz series P-Valley in 2021.

They began dating in June 2021 and announced they were expecting their first child together in May 2022.

Watts gave birth to a son, Xi Leì Lepley, on October 27, 2022.

Lepley is known for his roles in the films The Haves and the Have Nots and The Perfect Match, as well as the television series Grown-ish and The Game.

The couple has been open about their relationship on social media, sharing photos and videos of their travels, adventures, and family life.

The two have also been vocal about their support for each other’s careers.

In an interview with Essence, Lepley said of Watts;

“She’s the most beautiful woman I’ve ever seen, inside and out. She’s my best friend, my lover, and the mother of my child. I’m so grateful to have her in my life.”

Lepley and Watts are a power couple in the entertainment industry, and they are sure to continue to be successful in their careers.

They are also a loving couple, and they are raising their son together in a happy and supportive home.
