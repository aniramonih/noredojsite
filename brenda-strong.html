Desperate Housewives' Actress, Brenda Strong Is a Married Twice. Know Her Marital Life

Posted by Patria Henriques on Monday, August 12, 2024

Two-time Emmy-nominated American actress, Brenda Strong is best known for her role Mary Alice Young in the popular television series, Desperate Housewives. It aired on ABC from October 3, 2004, until May 13, 2012, for eight seasons.

She also starred as Nora Walker in Netflix's 13 Reasons Why from 2018 to 2020. As of now, she portrays her voice work as Narrator in the documentary TV series Blood Relatives, on Investigation Discovery. To know more about Brenda read the entire article. 

Got Married With John Farmanesh-Bocca in 2015

61, Brenda Strong has been married twice in her life. For the second time, she has tied the wedding knot with her long time boyfriend, John Farmanesh-Bocca on 2 May 2015. He is a film theatre producer who has spent three decades of his career in the theatre.

They said Yes I Do secretly and has kept their wedding details away from the limelight. As per E-News!, the newlywed's duo spent their romantic honeymoon at Villa del Palmar in Loreto, Mexico. On 2nd May 2021, she wished her husband a first-year marriage anniversary through her Instagram post by uploading the snaps of two. "Happy Iron Anniversary to my love, ", she wrote.

 Before their nuptials, they became engaged in January 2015 after few years of dating. Supergirl's actress said Closer Weekly that she honestly never thought she would find somebody else that she could spend the rest of her life with. 

Regardless, they met while working on a play, but they didn't start dating until two years later.

Failed Marriage With Tom Henri

7th Heaven alum was previously hitched to her first husband, Tom Henri for nearly 24 years. They began dating in 1988. Finally, the lovebirds exchanged the vows on 8th Jul 1989 in an intimate ceremony, following one year of the love relationship.

However, their marital relationship ended in divorce after Strong filed for divorce from Henri on January 14, 2011. They officially ended their marriage in Jun 2013 after being married for 23 years and 11 months.

Together, they had an adult son Zak Henri, born on 5 November 1994. Now, she's following his mother footsteps and has already acted in several TV shows namely Blended, Bunheads and Awkward, just like Sonja Kinski.

Strong who started her acting career in the mid-1980s has made $175 thousand per episode from her role Mary Alice Young in Desperate Housewives, opposite Felicity Huffman. Her other guest and supporting roles include Dallas, Gilmore Girls, The 100, Fear The Walking Dead, and Supergirl. 

Also, she directed season four of the Netflix series, 13 Reasons Why starring Matthew Alan, and Katherine Langford. Along with this, she has appeared in films such as Ocean of Pearls, Spaceballs, Starship Troopers, Spaceballs, and more. Prior to acting, she became 'Miss Arizona and Miss America.
