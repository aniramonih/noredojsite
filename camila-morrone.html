Camila Morrone Biography, Age, Height, Husband, Net Worth

Posted by Billy Koelling on Sunday, August 11, 2024

Camila Morrone is a young, talented, and beautiful actress and model. She has worked in many films like Never Goin’ Back, Mickey and the Bear, Valley Girl, and many more. Apart from her acting career, she was also seen on the runway as a model for Moschino’s resort collection. In 2016 she was on the cover of the Vogue magazine Turkey edition.

She gained widespread acclaim when her name was linked with legendary actor Leonardo DiCaprio. Recently she starred in the new Amazon Prime Video television miniseries Daisy Jones and The Six where she portrayed the role of Camilla Dunne. 

Early Life

Camila Morrone was born on 16th June 1997 in Los Angeles, California, United States of America. Her mother’s name is Lucila Polak, belongs to the Slovenian and Ashkenazi Jewish community her father is Maximo Morrone who works as a model and belongs of Italian, Romanian, and Spanish descent. In the year 2006, her parents filed for divorce and her mother was related to Al Pacino who is an American actor and filmmaker. She considers Al Pacino as her stepfather.  According to sources she did her schooling at Beverly Hills High School. There is no information shared about her further studies but since she was born and raised in Los Angeles like others she wanted to build up her career in Hollywood. She has also attended the Al Pacino School of Acting.

Camila Morrone Career

As her father and stepfather were already in the Hollywood business which also gave her an entry into the same. In 2013 she made her debut by working as an actress with James Franco in a film named Bukowski. Three years after she made her debut, she got into a modeling career. In 2016 she was on the cover page of the Turkey edition of Vogue and in 2017, she made her debut as a runway model for Moschino’s resort collection.

In 2018 she was seen in a film named Never Goin’ Back with Augustine Frizzell and also in Death Wish where her character’s name was Jordan Kersey. She also worked in a film named Mickey and the Bear where her role was Mickey Peck in 2019.  In 2020 her film Valley Girl was released which is an American Jukebox musical romantic film directed by Rachel Lee Goldenberg. Recently she has also been cast in Daisy Jones & The Six which is an upcoming Amazon series.
