Career

During the initial years of his career, he worked as a desk assistant as well as a researcher for ABC News. He also worked in the presidential campaign of Bill Clinton in 1992 as a producer. Two years later, he became a producer with the special events unit of ABC Network in New York and later claimed the post of an editorial producer. In 1997, he took over the position of the political director for ABC News. He has also founded the newsletter called The Note, which appears every weekday on ABCNews.com, which is an analysis of political as well as trending news of the world.

Mark and John F. Harris together published their first book named The Way to Win: Clinton, Bush, Rove in 2006 and another in 2008, called, How to Take the White House. He has been the board member of the New Hampshire Institute of Politics, since 2006 and he is an active member of their advisory board to date. Time magazine also gave him the post of a senior political analyst. In 2011, Time released an iPad app named “Mark Halperin 2012,” which contained materials from his “The Page” and also videos, photos, and his take on the news.
