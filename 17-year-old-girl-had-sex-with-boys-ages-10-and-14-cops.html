Girl, 17, had sex with boys ages 10 and 14 while other kids watched: cops

Posted by Tandra Barner on Thursday, August 22, 2024

Explore More

A 17-year-old Mississippi girl had sex with two boys ages 10 and 14 in front of other kids at a Mother’s Day gathering — then arranged to have the children who blabbed about it beaten up, according to cops.

Amiracle Morgan was busted last week after some of the kids reported her to cops, Columbus Police Chief Joseph Daughtry told WTVA.

“It was disturbing, it really was,” the police chief said of the depraved incident.

During police interviews, Morgan confessed to sex acts with both underage boys and was charged as an adult with sexual battery, cops said. Initial charges of statutory rape were later dropped, WTVA said, without explanation.

At some point, Morgan and her 32-year-old mom, Raven Morgan, got a group of kids to attack those who squawked to cops, Daughtry told WTVA.

“It was also a little more disturbing as well when I found out the mother brought her children to beat up the people who saw what took place. That’s not going to be tolerated,” Daughtry told WTVA.

“Nobody should have to worry about people coming jumping on them, breaking in doors and wanting to fight them,” the police chief said.

“And for what? Because you did wrong?” the top cop said.

“Mama act the fool, she going to jail, too. Everybody goes to jail,” he said.

The mom was charged with contributing to the delinquency of a minor, a misdemeanor.

Her daughter has been released on a $100,000 bond pending a preliminary hearing June 8.
