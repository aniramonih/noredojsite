Who is Boywithuke?

BoyWithUke is a multi-talented artist hailing from South Korea and the United States. His unique characteristic is his decision to conceal his identity, particularly his face. His journey to stardom began on the social media platform TikTok, where he captured the hearts of many with his hit track "Two Moons." Subsequently, he skyrocketed to fame with songs like "Toxic" and "Understand," solidifying his status as one of TikTok's most popular faceless artists.
