Early Life

Stanley Martin Lieber was the son of Jack Lieber and Celia, who gave birth to him in 1922 in the state of New York City. He grew up in Manhattan together with his brother Larry Lieber.

Stan attended DeWitt Clinton High School in 1939 and enrolled in WPA Federal Theatre Project. When he was growing up during his high school years, he used to do some part-time jobs such as writing press releases and obituaries, being an office boy, and a delivery man.

Lee was a Jew since he was raised in a Jewish family. He met Joan Clayton Boocock, whom he married in 1947 and had together the couple had one daughter. The wife of 95 years just died recently 2017 from stroke-related complications.

Age, Height, and Weight

Being born on 28 December 1922, Stan Lee was 95 years old at the time of his death. His height was 1.8 m. tall, and weigh 82 kg.

Career

Lee started off his career by being an employee at Martin Goodman’s company, where he was an assistant at a new Timely Comics division, a subsection of Pulp magazine. He started off by writing Captain America fillers then went ahead to create the first superhero Destroyer in Mystic Comics around 1941.

During this time of the Golden age of comic books, he created Father Time and Jack Frost. In 1941 due to disagreements with the company, he threatened to leave and was offered the position of editor-in-chief till 1972 when he became a publisher.

His marvel revolution kicked started when he was given an opportunity by Martin Goodman, the publisher, to work with a group of the superhero team. His first creation, together with artist Jack Kirby was Fantastic Four.

Again working with Kirby, he created Hulk, Thor, Doctor Strange, X -Men, Spider-Man, Iron Man, and Daredevil, whom they all called The Avengers. He wrote many monthly comic books, and last of it before becoming a publisher were Fantastic Four issue 125 dated 1972 august and The Amazing Spider-Man issue number 110 dated 1972 July.

Apart from writing Lee had made some cameo appearances in numerous Marvel films and television shows such as X-Men: Pryde of the X-Men as a narrator, he produced animated TV series of Spider-Man 1990 and Hulk and the Agents of S.M.A.S.H. on marvel films his voice was used in animation Big Hero 6 2014 as the father of Fred.

Other cameo appearances on films are in X-Men 2000, Spider-Man 2002, Daredevil 2003, Hulk 2003, Fantastic Four 2005, Iron Man 2008, Thor 2011, Guardians of the Galaxy Vol 2 2017, Spider-Man Home Coming 2017, Thor Ragnarok 2017, and he will make a cameo appearance in Deadpool 2 in 2018.

Awards & Achievements

Lee had received a number of awards for his works in the comic industry. Some of these awards are induction in Jack Kirby Hall of Fame, and Will Eisner award Hall of Fame. He was a recipient of the National Medal of Arts, Hugo Award, Inkpot Award, Comic-Con Icon Award, and a star on Hollywood Walk of Fame. In addition, The County of Los Angeles and City of Long Beach declared October 2 as Stan Lee Day
