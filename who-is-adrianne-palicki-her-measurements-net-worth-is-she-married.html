Who is Adrianne Palicki?

She was born Adrianne Lee Palicki on the 6th day of May 1983 in Toledo, Ohio, United States of America. Palicki is of mixed ancestry: Hungarian and Polish on her father’s side and English and German on her mother’s side. She is one of two children born to Nancy Lee (née French) and Jeffrey Arthur Palicki and has an older brother, Eric, who is a comic book writer. It was his brother who sparked his interest in comics.

Adrianne attended Whitmer High School where she acted in plays in her sophomore year. Besides performing in plays, she was also good at sports; she played basketball, ran track and was also a finalist for homecoming queen.
