Bugs Beddow Obituary: Final Goodbye to Beautiful Soul

The music industry and the Detroit gang mourned the passing of a true legend, Bugs Beddow on Tuesday, September 12, 2023. At the age of 68, Bugs Beddow lost his courageous and protracted battle with life leaving behind a lasting legacy.

The gap left by Bugs Beddow’s passing cannot possibly be filled, but those who were fortunate enough to experience his genius will forever carry his music and soul in their hearts.
