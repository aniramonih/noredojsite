China McClain of 'House of Payne' Flaunted Curves in Golden Dress &amp; Matching Crown in New Pics

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, August 19, 2024

China McCain looked stunning in her Halloween costume, and guess who went as her character in "Descendants 2?"

While you might not familiar with the name China Anne McClain, you will surely remember Chyna Parks from the Disney show, "A.N.T. Farm."

China Anne McClain attends the REVOLT X AT&T Host REVOLT Summit In Los Angeles at Magic Box | Photo: Getty Images

China played that role among many others, including "Gabby Harrison" from the comedy show, "How to Build a Better Boy" and Chris Rock's daughter in the 2010 hit, "Grown-ups," as revealed by The Famous People.

To take you further down memory lane, she was the primary artist of the Pop and R&B girl brand, "McClain."

China Anne McClain attends Entertainment Weekly's Comic-Con Bash held at FLOAT, Hard Rock Hotel San Diego | Photo: Getty Images

Recently, China shared a series of photos of her Halloween costume in which she looked effortlessly elegant. She appeared like an amazon in her warlike golden dress and matching crown.

Her eulogistic caption read:

"if God decided to transform the sun into melanin itself for a day. Happy Halloween☀️"

In the first frame, China knelt with her eyes closed, looking like she was absorbing the beauty of nature, in subsequent snapshots, she showed off her costume from different angles.

Since its upload, the post has gained over 600k likes and 3,757 comments. China is indeed everything an amazon represents, as she is strong-willed and ambitious. Not only is she focused on her career, but she takes her academics seriously as well.

China began her professional career at age 7, and finished from high school with honors two years ago, TVOM noted. She was discovered by a music director in 2005 when "The Gospel" was still auditioning,.

The music executive used his influence to get the movie director, Rob Hardy, to give China a shot and from there, China became a big star who even high-profile celebrities love.

This was evident when a Hollywood A-lister dressed as her character for Halloween.

For the spooky celebration, Kerry Washington went as a character from "Descendants 2," Uma, the daughter of the sea witch Ursula, Just Jared shared. Luckily, it was China that portrayed that character.

Kerry uploaded a clip of her costume with a caption that read:

"?I call the shots, you know who I am?. #HappyHalloween and thank you to @chinamcclain for the costume inspiration. I am obsessed with this @descendants cast and these movies!!!!! #MomLife ?."

China saw how Kerry honored her and

"What’s Her Name?? @kerrywashington!! We are obsessed with YOU!!! You are rockin’ that costume to the highest level???? #HappyHallowen."

This Halloween would no doubt be a memorable one for China!
