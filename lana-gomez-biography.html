Who is Lana Gomez?

Lana Gomez is a renowned American artist. Lana owns Wearstler’s design studio for a year from 2008 to 2009.

She came to fame after marrying a comedian, Sebastian Maniscalco.

Lana Gomez- Parents, Age(40), Ethnicity, Education

Lana Gomez was born on 23rd April 1983 in the United States of America.

Her mother’s name is Simone Lutgert Gomez who is a philanthropist and her father’s name is Scott Lutgert.

Her ethnicity is European and her nationality is Spanish. She grew up with her siblings Heather Dockweiler and Rodney Weiss.

She graduated from the University of Tennessee and studied art.

Lana Gomez- Professional Life, Career

Lana Gomez is interested in arts from a very young age. To pursue her dream, she went to Los Angeles. Her work has been recognized by Kelly Wearstler, an interior designer.

Then they both worked together and made several collaborations.

From 2008-2009, Lana Gomez was a resident painter at Wearstler’s design studio. She did a mural-sized piece and has been featured in a few books.

In 2010, during the Sunset Strip in West Hollywood, she made a 10-foot guitar sculpture for Gibson GuitarTown.

Nowadays, Lana Gomez’s art like office buildings and private residences is loved and featured in many places including Greystone Mansion in Beverly Hills.

Her work has been featured in magazines like House Beautiful, InStyle, Lonny, Los Angeles Confidential, and The New Yorker.
