Is Brandon Blackstock Related To Reba McEntire?

Recently, the question of the relationship between the American singer and manager has arisen, and many are curious to know if they are related to each other or not.

Despite this, the marriage did not work out for the couple. However, the musician has a close bond with her three stepchildren.
