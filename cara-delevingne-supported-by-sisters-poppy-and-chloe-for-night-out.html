Cara Delevingne enjoys glitzy night out with family

Posted by Kelle Repass on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

    Cara Delevingne was surrounded by close friends and family at the launch of her new Love Your Lips campaign with YSL Beauté. The 22-year-old model was joined by sisters Chloe and Poppy, as well as father Charles for the glitzy night out. Cara, who is currently the face of YSL Beauté, upped the glam factor for the evening with a bold make-up look. The British beauty stole the show with dramatic black smokey eyes teamed with a statement red lipstick for a bold and glamorous effect.

    Cara Delevingne was supported by her family for the launch of the new campaign

    The young model continued to show off her style credentials in an all-black ensemble consisting of a fringed minidress, blazer and chunky heeled shoes. As well as spending some quality time with her father and sisters, Cara was spotted mingling with the star-studded guestlist which included Pixie Lott, Lilah Parsons and Henry Holland. The young model also took the opportunity to show off her musical talent, surprising party goers as she hopped onstage for a performance with the band.

    Cara rocked an all-black ensemble and bold make-up look for the evening

    Meanwhile, older sisters Poppy and Chloe showed that style runs in the family, holding their own in fashion-forward outfits. Poppy, who had been in Milan with husband James Cook the night before, chose a laidback chic outfit as she supported her younger sister. The 28-year-old fashionista rocked a pair of bright red trousers for a pop of colour, which she paired with a grey sleeveless vest, black fringed Chanel bag and black leather stilettos. Meanwhile Chloe kept out the winter chill with a stylish, multi-coloured and striped fluffy coat and showed off her enviably slender figure with black skinny leggings, completing the look with a pair of black stilettos. The stylish sisters are taking care of father Charles following the sad news that their paternal grandmother has passed away, aged 102. It seems that the evening did lift Charles' spirits as he was all smiles while he enjoyed quality time with his daughters.
