How Much Does it Cost to Replace a Steering Column?

Posted by Tandra Barner on Tuesday, August 13, 2024

The steering column is an essential component in the steering system of any vehicle as it connects the steering wheel to the steering box and helps in controlling the direction of the vehicle. Therefore, it is critical to ensure that your steering column is in proper working order. However, over time, the steering column can undergo wear and tear or even incur damages due to an accident. In such cases, it may be necessary to replace the steering column entirely. But how much does it cost to replace a steering column? Let’s dive in to find out.

Factors Affecting the Cost of Steering Column Replacement

The cost of replacing a steering column can vary widely depending on several factors, including:

Vehicle Make and Model

The make and model of your vehicle play a significant role in determining the cost of steering column replacement. Some vehicles are more expensive to repair due to the complexity of their steering systems.

Location of Repair Shop

Another crucial factor influencing the cost of steering column replacement is the geographic location of the repair shop. Repair shops in urban areas or high-end neighborhoods may charge more than those in suburban or rural areas.

Type of Steering Column Replacement

The cost of steering column replacement also depends on the type of replacement required. You can either replace the entire steering column or specific parts of the column that have experienced damage. The latter option can be less expensive.

Cost Range for Steering Column Replacement

The average cost of steering column replacement ranges from $300 to $1,000, depending on the factors mentioned above. However, the cost can go up or down depending on the complexity of the repair and the make and model of your vehicle. In some cases, the cost can even exceed $1,000.

If you are looking to replace your steering column, it is recommended to get a written estimate from a reputable repair shop. This way, you can compare the costs across various shops and choose the one that suits your budget.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I replace a steering column on my own?

Replacing a steering column can be a complicated and time-consuming task that requires a high level of mechanical expertise. It is not recommended for a novice to attempt steering column replacement on their own. It is always best to take your vehicle to a professional mechanic to ensure that the job is done correctly.

What are the typical symptoms of a damaged steering column?

Some of the common indicators of a damaged steering column include difficulty in turning the steering wheel, a squeaking or grinding noise from the column, or if the steering wheel feels loose.

What are the benefits of replacing a steering column?

Replacing a damaged steering column can ensure that your vehicle is safe to drive. It can also help in improving your vehicle’s steering performance.

Can I drive my vehicle without a steering column?

No, it is not safe to drive a vehicle without a steering column.

Can I repair a damaged steering column?

In some cases, you may be able to repair a damaged steering column by replacing individual parts. However, it is best to consult a professional mechanic to assess the extent of the damage and provide you with the appropriate repair options.

How can I prolong the life of my steering column?

The best way to extend the life of your steering column is to ensure that you maintain your vehicle regularly. Regular maintenance can decrease the likelihood of damage to your steering column.

Can I replace specific parts of the steering column?

Yes, in some cases, you may only need to replace specific parts of the steering column rather than the entire column. This option can be less expensive.

Is it possible to lower the cost of steering column replacement?

Yes, you may be able to lower the cost of steering column replacement by getting multiple estimates from different repair shops and choosing the one that offers the most competitive pricing.

How long does it take to replace a steering column?

The time required to replace a steering column can vary depending on the make and model of your vehicle and the extent of the damage. However, it typically takes several hours to complete the job.

What should I do if I suspect my steering column is damaged?

If you suspect that your steering column is damaged, it is best to take your vehicle to a professional mechanic immediately. Continued use of the vehicle with a damaged steering column can lead to more significant issues.

Can the steering column be damaged in an accident?

Yes, the steering column can sustain damage due to an accident or collision.

What is the best way to find a reliable repair shop for steering column replacement?

To find a reliable repair shop for steering column replacement, you can ask for recommendations from friends and family or consult online reviews. It is also advisable to choose a repair shop that has certified mechanics and warranties its work.
