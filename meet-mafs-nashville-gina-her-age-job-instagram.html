Are MAFS Nashville Gina And Clint Still Together?

Hopeless romantics, MAFS Nashville Gina and Clint didn’t seem like the couple who’d give up their chance at love easily. However, the two remained silent about their relationship after filming the show.

Finally, four years later, she was then ready to begin her search again and willingly devoted herself to the experimental dating process. She had faith in the experts that they’d find her a man with whom she can dream, travel, and potentially build a family.

Likewise, her MAFS partner, Clint always wanted to have a family and loved the idea of having children of his own. So, despite being in his 40s and unable to find the one, he put his faith in MAFS experts. He believed that to have anything done right, you have to learn and listen to experts.

To summarize, they looked like the perfect pair.
