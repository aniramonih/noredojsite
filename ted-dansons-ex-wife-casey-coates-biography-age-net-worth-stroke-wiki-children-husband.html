Early Life & Education

Casey Coates, a woman fueled by a passion for creativity, was born in 1938 on the picturesque Long Island, New York. She grew up in an environment enriched by her father’s musical talents and her mother’s artistic abilities. As a child, she demonstrated her inherent creativity by constantly transforming her bedroom and generating imaginative ideas. Her artistic pursuits extended to painting on bare wood, showcasing her talent from a young age.

Continuing her educational journey, Casey Coates attended Great Neck High School before deciding to cultivate her skills at Lasell University in Boston. Seeking new experiences and opportunities abroad, she boldly chose to spend a year in London. However, driven by a deep connection to her roots, she eventually returned to her beloved New York.

In 1975, Casey Coates earned her Bachelor of Fine Arts in environmental design from Parsons School of Design, graduating with honors. She is renowned for her designs of solar-powered homes, a facet of her work prominently featured in the pages of Angels Unawares.

Career

As a mother of two deeply committed to environmental stewardship, Casey Coates took the initiative to establish her nonprofit organization, Global Possibilities. As a national advocate for solar and renewable energy, she tirelessly promotes using sustainable energy sources.

Personal Life

Ted Danson and Casey Coates tied the knot on December 24, 1977, after meeting in 1976. Their union welcomed a daughter in 1979, and they expanded their family by adopting another daughter in 1985.

Despite 16 years of marriage, the Dansons faced challenges in the late 1980s and early 1990s, leading to the dissolution of their marriage due to adultery. Ted Danson‘s involvement with actress Whoopi Goldberg prompted Casey Coates to file for divorce. Their daughter, Kate Danson, has pursued a diverse career as a doula, actress, and photographer. The couple’s other adoptive child, Alexis Danson, prefers to maintain anonymity.

Before marrying Casey Coates, Ted Danson had previously married and divorced actress Randy Danson in 1975. Currently, Ted is married to Mary Steenburgen, also an actress.
