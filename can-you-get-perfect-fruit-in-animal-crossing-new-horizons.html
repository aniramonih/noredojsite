Can you get Perfect Fruit in Animal Crossing: New Horizons?

Posted by Kelle Repass on Tuesday, August 6, 2024
Recommended Videos

Perfect Fruit are a traditional item in the Animal Crossing series, and people are wondering if they can get Perfect Fruit in New Horizons. The good news is, yes, you can. Native fruit trees can produce perfect fruit, so you should always make sure you have plenty of fruit trees on your island.

While the chances of a Perfect Fruit are rare, they are very valuable. If you want, you can sell them to the Nooks for 600 Bells, which is quite the increase from the 100 Bells you get for standard native fruit.

The best thing to do with a Perfect Fruit is to plant it, and this will cause a Perfect Fruit Tree to grow. This tree will regularly produce Perfect Fruit, which you can then sell to the Nooks for lots of Bells. How many fruit trees, or perfect varieties, you want is up to you. To help you plan, we will list below all the prices you can get for different types of fruit from the Nooks.

  • Native Fruit – 100 Bells
  • Foreign Fruit from another players island – 500 Bells
  • Perfect Fruit – 600 Bells
  • Foreign Perfect Fruit – 3000 Bells

As you can see, it is a great idea to trade perfect fruit with other players, so you can both make plenty of Bells from the Nooks, so make sure to coordinate with friends to take advantage of this.
