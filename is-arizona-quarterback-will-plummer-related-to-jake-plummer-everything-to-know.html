Is Will Plummer Related To Jake Plummer?

No, Will Plummer is not related to Jake Plummer.

Jason Steven Plummer, professionally recognized as Jake Plummer, is an American professional former football player who was in the NFL for 10 seasons.

Well, it seems obvious why people think they are related because they are both in a similar professional field and they have the same surname.

Some even say that they might be father and son until they find out the truth, as mentioned earlier, that they are in no way related to each other.

Who Is Will Plummer Brother?

Jack Plummer is the elder brother of college football player Will Plummer, and interestingly, he is also a professional footballer.

He is currently in his junior year of college, about 2 years older than his younger brother Will, and he plays for the Purdue Boilermakers as a quarterback.
