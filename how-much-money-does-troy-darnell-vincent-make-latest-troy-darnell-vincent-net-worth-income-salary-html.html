Troy Darnell Vincent (born June 8, 1970) is a former American football cornerback who played for the National Football League’s Miami Dolphins, Philadelphia Eagles, Buffalo Bills, and Washington Redskins (NFL). In the 1992 NFL Draft, the Dolphins selected him with the 7th overall choice. He was a member of the Wisconsin football team in college.

Vincent is the NFL executive vice president of football operations, formerly the NFL Player Development Organization, which he was appointed to in February 2010.

He was the NFLPA’s president, The Sporting News’ No. 1 Good Guy in 2003, and a team captain for the final 13 years of his 15-year career. Vincent was recognised as one of the NFL Hall of Fame Class of 2012’s Preliminary Nominees on September 28, 2011.
