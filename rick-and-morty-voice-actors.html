Rick and Morty Voice Actors: A Guide of the Cast in the Animated Sci-Fi Sitcom

Posted by Tandra Barner on Wednesday, August 21, 2024

Rick and Morty Voice Actors: The world of the eccentric weirdos of “Rick and Morty” has expanded over the period. Exploring through different galaxies, planets, timelines and of course multiverses, the fans have been introduced to the strange characters, creatures and people in this series. Ricky and his grandson Morty prove to be the perfect duo for discovering new weird things, triggering endless crimes and even saving the universe.

The show debuted back on 2nd December 2013 and it has been a success since then. “Rick and Morty” Season 6 dropped back on 4th September 2022. But who are the “Rick and Morty” voice actors that led to the immense success of the animated series? Here are the Rick and Morty voice actors and the famous characters.

Here is the list of Rick and Morty Voice Actors

Justin Roiland As Rick, Morty, Mr. Meseeks, Mr. Always Wants to Be Hunted & Mr. Poopyutthole

You thought Rick and Morty are voiced by different actors? Well, you are definitely wrong. Apart from co-creating the show, he is the Rick and Morty voice actor behind the two main leads of the show. 

Rick is a genius who has nihilistic personal views as well as uncontrolled alcoholism. His mental health problems may have stemmed from spending time discovering, traveling through portals and troubled lifestyles. Moreover, he has a very controversial outlook on the conventional lifestyle. He kept on tagging his grandson Morty in his crazy adventures. In spite of all of these, he has a heart of gold and ends up helping the people whom he cares about.

Now, when it comes to Mortimer “Morty” Smith Sr., he is an unlucky grandson who never had the choice but to accompany his grandpa on his expeditions. Morty is a student at Harry Herpson High School. He started as an anxious individual but in no time, he had to face his fears to a part of Rick’s adventures.

Apart from being “Rick and Morty’s” voice actor, Roiland has been doing the same for years now. He is famous for “House of Cosbys”, “2 Girls One Cup: The Show” and many other prominent roles. You can also read about Rick and Morty Season 6 here.

Spencer Grammer as Summer Smith

Spencer has remained constant as the “Rick and Morty” voice actor and she has been involved in live-action work mainly. She has been in shows like “Grey’s Anatomy”, “Chicago PD”, “CSI” and more. With her character of Summer, she becomes a part of the comical grandad-grandson’s antics.

She is intelligent, have quite similarities with her grandfather and sometimes ends up being jealous of Rick. Originally, she wanted to stay away from the misadventures but since she is just like any typical American teen girl looking for popularity, she chooses to be included in their journey. Besides, she also wishes that her family could become normal. 

Sarah Chalke as Beth Smith

The daughter of Rick and mother to Morty, Beth Smith is a horse surgeon who is the main breadwinner supporting her loved family. She is a talented vet but her insecurities make her feel that she isn’t an actual veterinarian or a “real doctor”. Moreover, her husband does not contribute to the household and she also suffers from huge abandonment issues after her father left her alone for 20 years.

On the other hand, Sarah has previously done comic roles like that in “Roseanne”, “Clone High” and “American Dad”. The Canadian actress is well-respected in the industry.

Chris Parnell as Jerry Smith

Rose to fame with his character of Garth Holliday in “Anchorman: The Legend of Ron Burgundy”. Later he entered into the world of voicing with “Archer”, “Gravity Falls”, “Robot Chicken” and more before being a “Rick and Morty” voice actor. 

Jerry and Beth were high school fling and he got Beth pregnant on the couple’s prom night. After that, they married and had their second child. The couple feels like their teen pregnancy and early marriage have forced them to miss out on many things.

They do share a strained relationship and he does not have any career plans. Despite not agreeing on supporting Rick’s antics he doesn’t have much to say about Morty joining his grandfather.

Well, these are the main “Rick and Morty” voice actors who have remained in the show since its inception. There are always new additions to the list of “Rick and Morty” voice actors as most of the characters have guest appearances. However, the OG cast can never be replaced and that is why “Rick and Morty” voice actors will always reside in the fans’ hearts.
