David Faber Son: Everything On CNBC Analyst Children &amp; Family

Posted by Kelle Repass on Wednesday, August 21, 2024

Who is American financial journalist David Faber son? Learn everything about the CNBC news analyst’s family and married life.

David Faber is an American financial journalist.

He is also the market news analyst for CNBC. Currently, he co-hosts CNBC’s morning show Squawk on the Street. Additionally, he hosts the show Business Nation. And he is also serving as the guest host on Jeopardy!.

Aside from journalism, David is also the author of the books, The Faber Report (2002), Roof Caved In (2009), and House of Cards: The Origins of the Collapse (2010).

Further, he has earned the 2005 Peabody Award and Alfred I. DuPont-Columbia University Award.

David Faber Son: Everything On His Children And Family

David Faber is reported to have a son.

However, the identity of his son is yet revealed to the general public. The same can be said regarding his daughter. It looks as if he likes to keep his children away from the spotlight.

And, talking about his marriage, Faber has been married to his wife Jenny Harris, a business journalist/television producer. His wife is also the fraternal twin sister of musician Jesse Harris and a graduate of Cornell University.

Although little is known about their dating life, the couple got married on 16 January 2000.

More about his family, his in-laws are lawyer Jay Harris and As the World Turns actress Marie Masters. His father-in-law Jay worked as the counsel for Hall Dickler Kent Friedman & Wood and is known for producing Side Man.

As for David’s own parents, they are Norman L. Faber and Belle B. Faber. Not much information has been made available on the two.

David Faber Net Worth

David Faber’s net worth amounts to $16 million, according to CelebrityNetWorth.

The site has also deemed his annual salary at $4 million.

Faber certainly has a boastful income. Well, considering his 25 year-long successful journalism career, it isn’t surprising at all. A renowned journalist, he definitely had some profitable contracts.

While talking about David’s wealth, his wife also seems to have an individual net worth of about $250 thousand. Although not verified, his brother-in-law Jesse seems to have a whopping net worth of $20 million.

David may be worth even more. His career hasn’t halted, and he is likely to earn more fortune in the upcoming future.
