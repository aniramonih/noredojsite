Meet Spencer Rattlers Parents Susan and Mike Rattler

Posted by Kelle Repass on Tuesday, August 20, 2024

Spencer Rattler’s parents are Susan Konkel Rattler (mother, born March 17, 1970) and Michael Rattler (father, born in the late 1960s). The University of South Carolina Gamecocks’ quarterback, Spencer Rattler (born September 28, 2000), does not need much of an introduction himself.

His mother Susan is a 2nd-grade teacher while his father Mike is a former football coach who currently works as a regional sales rep under the employ of Citizens Automobile and Recreation Finance. Spencer Rattler’s parents have played a significant role in his seismic rise to become one of the biggest NFL prospects in the country.

Given that he appears to be a few years older than Spencer’s mother, who was born in the early 1970s, it would not be too far off to assume that he was born in the late 1960s.

The identity of his parents or any other family member is also shrouded in mystery, but given his skin color, one could say that they are 100 per cent African American. Also, very little is known about his formal educational background, but there have been a lot of sources who claim that Mike Rattler played football in high school as well.

Spencer Rattler is Not The Only Child of His Parents
