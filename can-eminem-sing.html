Can Eminem Sing?

Posted by Tandra Barner on Monday, August 26, 2024

He may be one of the world’s best rappers, but can Eminem sing too? 

Eminem is not only a talented rapper, but the artist can sing too. While he’s mostly known for his rapping abilities, Eminem can and does sing—and the world happens to think that he’s pretty good at it. 

While some critics would disagree, most of the world is on board with Eminem’s singing abilities. His true talent lies in rapping, but that doesn’t mean the artist can’t belt out a hook, or bridge, or entire song if he wants to. 

He’s Not Afraid

Marshall Bruce Mathers III, or who we know as Eminem, is one of the world’s most talented rappers. Ever since he released his first album back in 1996, he’s taken the world by storm with his talented (and somewhat controversial) songs. 

While he reportedly doesn’t like singing (he actually said that he knows he can’t sing very well), many fans enjoy when he sings for part or a whole song.

Sing For The Moment

Eminem has several songs that are uncharacteristically heavy on the singing—not the rapping. 

Many fans really like the song Stronger Than I Was, Twisted, and Crazy In Love. Hallie’s Song is another good one, as is Puke. 

When it boils down to it, you either like Eminem’s singing voice or you don’t. It’s hard to argue against the fact that he’s one of the world’s best rappers, but some people wouldn’t agree when that comes to his singing. 

However, if you were to ask most fans, they happen to love his unique singing voice. And we do too.
