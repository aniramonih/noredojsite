As an American national of white ethnicity, Luis was raised in a supportive environment by his parents, Iris Ruelas (mother) and Luis Ruelas Sr. (father), alongside his two siblings, Jennifer Ruelas and David Ruelas. 

Luis ‘Louie’ Ruelas attended Tappan Zee High School, where he discovered his passion for business and leadership, laying the foundation for his future success.

Career

Luis ‘Louie’ Ruelas boasts an impressive entrepreneurial background, having launched his first venture at 19 while working at Ripe Old. Although details of his other work experiences are scarce, his professional trajectory showcases his dedication and perseverance. 

In 2010, he assumed the CEO role at Interacting Marketing Solutions, where he spent nearly 13 years demonstrating his exceptional leadership skills and ability to drive growth and success.

After departing from his previous company in August 2012, Luis ‘Louie’ Ruelas co-founded Digital Media Solutions Group and serves as Executive Vice President of Business Development. He leverages his expertise in this role to propel company growth and expansion.

Beyond his professional accomplishments, Luis ‘Louie’ Ruelas is deeply committed to philanthropy, particularly supporting autistic children.
