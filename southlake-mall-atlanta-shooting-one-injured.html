Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, August 4, 2024

A SHOOTING at the Southlake Mall in Atlanta left shoppers terrified on June 10, 2021.

Authorities were called to the scene and locked down the area after gunfire was heard.

What is the shooting in Southlake mall Atlanta?

Shoppers fled the area around Atlanta's Southlake Mall after a shooting broke out.

Stores within the mall are reportedly still on lockdown although police say it is not an active shooter situation.

Fox5 reported that there was a white car with a broken back windshield in the parking lot.

The incident took place in a Macy's and spilled over into the parking area, CBS46 reports.

The investigation into the incident is being led by Morrow police.
