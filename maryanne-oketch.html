On Day Six, she discovered one of three Beware Idols that could only be given power through an activation phrase. It had to be used simultaneously with the activation phrases of the other two Beware Idols.

More about her career

Later, Maryanne told the jury about her gameplay at the Final Tribal Council, showing them the second immunity idol she had found and kept hidden from the rest of the tribe. She also explained her strategy for eliminating Zaheer at the Final Six.
