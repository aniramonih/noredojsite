Meet Preston Pippen, a son of Scottie Pippen and Larsa Pippen.

Posted by Billy Koelling on Sunday, August 4, 2024

The people who gain prominence right after their birth without doing any notable work are celebrity kids. Like numerous other celebrity children, Preston Pippen is also the one. 

Pippen is grabbing full media attention as one of the seventh kids of the legendary sports personality Scottie Pippen and his former wife, Larsa Younan Pippen.

Short Wiki

The celebrity son, Preston was born on August 26, 2002, in the United States of America, under the zodiac sign of Virgo. Preston currently enjoys his teenage with his lovely siblings and parents. 

Additionally, he studied at Sierra Canyon High School, located in Chatsworth, California. The 6-feet-1-inch (185 cm) tall Pippen also plays basketball for his school basketball team.

Who Are Larsa & Scottie Pippen?

The 18-year-old Preston's mother, Larsa Pippen, is an American model, and reality actress who has appeared in reality shows like The Real Housewives of Miami, Keeping Up with the Kardashians, etc. 

On the other side, Preston's father, Scotty Maurice Pippen, widely popular as Scottie, is one of the heroic names all over the sports world. The 55 aged Scottie is a retired basketball player who played for 11 years for the professional basketball team Chicago Bulls.

The 6th Times NBA Champion and many other prestigious awards-winning Scottie have also played for the teams such as Portland Trail Blazers (1999-2003), and Houston Rockets (1998-1999). The former Chicago Bulls player Pippen shares a good bond with another basketball legend Michael Jordan. 

How Is Preston's Parents' Relationship? Know About Their Divorce

The reality star Larsa and the former basketballer Scottie began their romantic affair in 1995. After staying in a relationship for almost three years, they exchanged the wedding vow in Chicago on July 10, 1997.

However, in 2016, after 19 years of the marital bond, Larsa and Scottie separated. As per TMZ, Larsa filed for divorce back in 2018. Even after the separation, Scottie and Larsa are sharing a healthy bond to raise their children jointly.

The divorce was finalized in 2021. The case was active for three years.

Scottie became a father for the first time in 1987 and shared a son, Antron Pippen, with Karen McCollum. Scottie and Karen were married from 1988 to 1990.

Likewise, Scottie is a father to Sierra Pippen (b. 1995). Sierra was born to Scootie's former fiancee Yvette De Leon.

Similarly, he and his then-girlfriend Sonya Roby shared twin daughters Taylor and Tyler Pippen in 1994. Taylor's sister Tyler died nine days after her birth. He only claimed paternity after a lawsuit.
