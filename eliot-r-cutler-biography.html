Who is Eliot R. Cutler?

Eliot R. Cutler is an American lawyer and businessman. He was an independent candidate for Governor of Maine.

Cutler served as a member of the U.S. Office of Management and Budget. He was recently arrested for his involvement in child pornography.

Eliot Cutler- Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity, Education

Eliot R. Cutler was born on 29 July 1946 in Bangor, Maine, America and belongs to a Jewish family.

He is the eldest son of Lawrence and Catherine Cutler. His father was a physician and his mother was an economist.

The American lawyer Eliot R. Cutler did his schooling at Deerfield Academy. Moreover, he graduated from Harvard College (1968) and ultimately earned a law degree from Georgetown Law Academy (1974).

Eliot Cutler-Professional Life, Career

Eliot R. Cutler started his career primarily as a legislative assistant to Senator Edmund Muskie. Next, he worked as an associate director for Natural Resources Energy and Science.

Cutler also worked for the law firm Webster and Sheffield for 8 long years.

Eliot R. Cutler was a founding partner of the second-largest environment law firm in the country, Cutler and Stanfield LLP.

He, then, launched The Gubernatorial Campaign twice in 2010 and 2014 but could not win once. Eliot was also involved in various political movements in his early days.

Rumors and Controversy
