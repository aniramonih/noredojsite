Jennifer Lopez Announced She Is Going On Tour

Posted by Billy Koelling on Sunday, August 25, 2024

It may be only February, but so far, this has been the year of Jennifer Lopez. Tomorrow, Lopez is releasing her first studio album since 2014 and an accompanying film titled This Is Me...Now: A Love Story, and next week, she is releasing a documentary, The Greatest Love Story Never Told, about the project's making. And as if that wasn't enough, Lopez just made another major announcement: She's going on tour.

On Thursday, the star appeared on TODAY to surprise fans with a tour this summer, aptly named the This Is Me...Now the Tour. According to the morning talk show, the tour, which kicks off in June, will visit more than 30 cities â€” including New York, Los Angeles, Miami, Toronto, and more. The tour marks her first since 2019 and will see her perform some of her greatest hits in addition to music from her new album.

"I will be going on tour this summer," she told Hoda Kotb, Savannah Guthrie, Al Roker, Craig Melvin, and Carson Daly during a segment outside of the studio in front of a cheering crowd. "We're hitting all the cities all across the United States."

"Going to do a bunch of new stuff that I'm really excited about," she added. "I've already seen the stage. It's going to be epic, I promise you that!"

General tickets will be on sale starting Feb. 22 at LiveNation.com. The J.Lo Fan Club, Citi, and Verizon pre-sales will be available on Feb. 20.
