Rick Newman Biography

Rick Newman is an American columnist for Yahoo Finance in New York City, covering business as well as economic trends, plus the 2016 presidential campaign, for the world’s largest financial news site. He is also a frequent commentator on CNN, Fox, MSNBC, ABC, and other TV as well as radio networks.

Prior to arriving at Yahoo in the year 2013, Rick was a chief business correspondent for U.S. News & World Report. Before that, Rick spent twelve years in Washington, where he covered the Pentagon for U.S. News as well as earned several awards, including the Gerald R. Ford Prize for Distinguished Reporting on National Defense.

His fourth book, Liberty for All: A Manifesto for Reclaiming Financial and Political Freedom, was published in the year 2015.

Rick’s the author of three other books: Rebounders: How Winners Pivot From Setback To Success (2012), Firefight: Inside the Battle to Save the Pentagon on 9-11 (2008) as well as Bury Us Upside Down: The Misty Pilots and the Secret Battle for the Ho Chi Minh Trail (2006). Newman has two kids in college and lives in Westchester County, NY.
