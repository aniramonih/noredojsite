Allira Cornell- Meet Eldest Daughter Of John Cornell and Delvene Delaney

Posted by Kelle Repass on Saturday, August 10, 2024

Allira Cornell is one of those TV personalities who came to the limelight because of her relationship with well-known stars. She is highly recognized as the daughter of Australian film producer, John Cornell and his actress wife, Delvene Delaney.

As per sources, Allira works as a photographer. Moreover, she was born in Mullumbimby, Australia to Johna and Delvene. However, her exact birth date has not revealed to the media. So far, she studied at Curtin University, where she received her bachelor degree in critical medical anthropology.

Is Allira Cornell Married?

The celebrity kid, Allira Cornell has lived a very low life since her early days. Neverthless, she has kept most of the details about her personal life hidden from the public and the media.

Thus there is still not known about her marital status at the current moment. According to sources, she is a happily married woman with her long term husband whose name remains hidden. From her beautiful marriage, she even has two children, Aelfie, 4, and Ayla, 13. 

Her kids are currently studying at Northern Rivers school. On the other hand, her dad, John and mom, Delvene, are in healthy marital life for 43 years. They first met on the set of The Paul Hogan Show in the 1970s and got married in 1977. Before her, the producer Cornell was married twice but his former wives details are under the warps.

Two Siblings- Liana and Melissa Cornell

Allira Cornell has two sisters named Liana Cornell, and Melissa Cornell. Liana, born on April 24, 1990, is her biological sister and Melissa, born in 1970, is her half-sister.

The graduate of NIDA in acting Liana is an actress who has acted in TV shows such as East of Everything, Britannia, Schapelle, and Wolf Creek. Moreover, she tied the knot with her husband, Dan Martinovich in December 2019 in a low-key wedding in the presence of family-only.
