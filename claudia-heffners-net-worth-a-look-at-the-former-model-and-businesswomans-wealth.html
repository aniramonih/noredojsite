Who is Claudia Heffner?

Born on March 12, 1955, in Baltimore, Maryland, Claudia Heffner is the daughter of William J. Heffner and Barbara Heffner.

She has two sisters, Holly McCloskey and Charlene Heffner.

Claudia received her education at a Catholic school in Baltimore and graduated with a bachelor’s degree in 1977.

Claudia embarked on her career as a fashion model in the late 1970s, collaborating with various talent agencies and distinguished fashion brands.

Her portfolio graced the covers of numerous magazines and editorials, as well as featured in advertising campaigns.

It was through her marriage to Nelson Peltz in 1985 that her modeling career reached new heights.

Opting to leave behind her modeling profession, Claudia redirected her focus towards family and supporting her husband’s business ventures.

Her contributions played a pivotal role in elevating the Peltz family’s business empire, now valued in the billions.

Their extensive holdings encompass diverse properties, investments, and companies across the United States.
