A glance at Oleg Basilashvili

Posted by Tandra Barner on Friday, August 30, 2024

Oleg Basilashvili is well known among the public as an actor. He is famous for his role as Yady in Sluzhebny Roman which was released in 1977. His full name is Oleg Valerianovich Basilashvili. He is 5feet 12 inches(181cm) tall.

He was born on September 26, 1934, in Moscow, Russia. His nationality is Russian and he holds Libra as his birth sign.

Who are Oleg's parents?

Valerian Basilashvili is his father who used to be a director at Moscow Polytechnic College. Irina Ilyinskaya is his mother who used to teach foreign languages.

Early life 

He was born in Moscow and raised in Georgia because of World War 2. He spent his childhood days staying away from his father and mother. He was taken care of by his parental grandfather in Georgia until the end of World War II. 

Education

Oleg completed his early education in Georgia and later he enrolled in the Acting School of the Moscow Art Theatre where his mentor used to be Pavel Massalsky and graduated from there in the year 1956. 

Professional Life and Career

He made his career from the scratch and he started his acting journey by portraying the role as a young groom in Nevesta which was written by Anton Chekhov. He has worked with many famous artists in his career such as stars as Kirill Lavrov, Tatyana Doronina, Alisa Freundlich, Lyudmila Makarova, Svetlana Kryuchkova, and many more.

He has done many hit movies and TV series by portraying various roles such as Rembrandt as Six, The Conspiracy of Ambassadors as George Lockhart, A Slave of Love as Savva Yuzhakov, Liquidation as The Old Man, and many more.

Awards

Oleg has been awarded People's Artist of the USSR in his career.

Oleg Political background

He was also elected as the representative of Leningrad in 1990 and after that, he became a member of the pro-democratic group of representatives in the Russian Parliament. He has also worked with Boris Yeltsin who was the Russian first president during his political career. 

But he did not continue his political career since he wanted to become an actor. By quitting his political career in the year 2000 he started to follow his passion and continued working in the film industry until this period of time.

Who is the current wife of Oleg?

The former Russian politician is currently staying in a married relationship with Galina Mshanskaya. The couple married in a private ceremony in the presence of family members and close friends. 

However, they have not disclosed their exact marriage date and other information regarding their love life. 

About his ex-wife and their marriage

He was once married to Tatiana Doronina who was an actress by profession. She was born on September 12, 1993, in Leningrad, Russia. She holds Virgo as her birth sign.

They started their relationship from friendship when they were colleagues. After dating each other for several months they tied their knot in 1955 in the presence of family members. However, their relationship could not last long and got divorced in 1963.
